Cookies
Our Service also uses cookies. These are text files containing information on your use of the Service (visited web pages, number of visits, time of the visits, time spent on individual pages, browser, operating system, etc.). The user profiles contained in these files are anonymized and analyzed in a technical (statistical) evaluation procedure. Thus, it is possible to display a selection of ads customized to the interests of the respective user when integrating online ads (such as banners) into other websites. We use pseudonymised cookies, each of which is assigned a random combination of numbers and/or numbers and letters, by means of which it is possible to determine that the user themselves is visiting the website. However, it is not possible to reveal the user’s identity with this combination of numbers. Such cookies are also used by our service providers.
Most of the cookies we use are so-called “session cookies”. They are deleted automatically once you have exited our website. Other cookies remain stored on your device until deleted by you. These cookies allow us to recognize your browser when you re-visit our website.
