Privacy Policy
How we collect data and why
When you vote on a game, we collect your IP address so that we can detect that you have voted and make it so that you cannot vote more than once.
In many of our games, we use cookies stored on your computer to save game data. This means when you exit the game and come back, all of your game data can be restored.
If you want to disable cookies, and this ability to save game data, you can do so by going into the preferences or settings menu on your browser.
We will never sell your data to anyone, mainly because nobody would ever want to buy or even see it. I don't want to see it. It just helps the website to run smoothly.
