Spear consulting doo - Spike Masters Volleyball - Privacy policy
About Spear privacy policy
Spear Consulting doo, company that made Spike Masters Volleyball mobile game does not collect, use or sell user data.
Information we receive and how we use it
Spike Masters mobile game is client-only app, and all user data is saved on user device. We use Google Play developer/Apple Developes pages, to track user statistics and behaviour.
In-app advertisment
Since our game is free, we use Google AdMob service to show ads and earn money. You are not obligated to click on them. On some devices it is possible to disable ads by disabling network traffic.
AdMob collects data and uses a unique identifier on your device to show relevant ads to you.
Google services operates under their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to check them in order to learn more about their data collecting practices.
In compliance with the GDPR(General Data Protection Regulation), users in the EEA(European Economic Area) will see a message asking for their consent
All game users can disable ads personalisation in the Settings menu
