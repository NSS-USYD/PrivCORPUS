What is a cookie?
A cookie is a small file for the storage and retrieval of information, generally consisting of alphanumeric characters (i.e. letters and numbers), distributed by a web server to the computer or electronic terminal of the user of the Site or our mobile applications to send status information to the browser and receive the same information back from the browser. Status information may comprise, for example, a session identifier, a language, an expiration date, a response domain, etc.
Cookies usually make it possible to obtain certain information about the browsing habits of the user, their computer or terminal, in order specifically to improve the content and service offered by the website or by the mobile application in question, to establish the level of traffic on the site or application in question, and to provide internet users with personalized services.
1.3       The term “the Cookies” as used herein covers all types of tracker or other similar technologies, such as:
HTTP cookies;
but also the use of other technologies:
locale shared objects, sometimes called “Flash cookies”;
local storage implemented in HTML 5;
device fingerprinting;
identifiers generated by operating systems (whether for advertising purposes or otherwise: IDFA, IDFV, Android ID, etc.);
hardware identifiers (MAC address, serial number, or other device identifier);
1.4       In addition, the Cookies can be:
session cookies which disappear when the user closes their browser or the website or the mobile game application, or
permanent cookies which remain until their lifetime validity expires or until the user deletes them using, for example, the functions of their browser (where this is possible) or via the cookie management module made available on our Site or on our mobile game applications.
What are the major categories of cookies?
There are two major categories of cookies that correspond to different legal regimes.
Functional cookies that are not subject to the user’s consent
Functional cookies are those the only purpose of which is to allow or facilitate communication by electronic means on, from or to the Site or mobile application in question, or which are strictly necessary for the provision of an online communication service at the express request of the user, that are exempt from the requirement for the publisher of the site or the mobile application in question to obtain the user’s consent for their installation.
We can use such functional cookies in this way, specifically technical cookies that are indispensable for browsing the site or using the mobile game applications, and cookies that are strictly necessary for providing certain services specifically requested by the user, without seeking your prior consent. If the user deactivates functional cookies, for example by using the functions offered by their browser or their terminal, they risk not being able to access the site or the mobile application in question, and/or not receiving the content and information available on the site or on the mobile application correctly, and/or not being able to benefit from all the functionality of the site or the mobile application .
Other cookies that are strictly subject to the user’s consent
The use of other cookies as described below is strictly subject to prior consent of the user, who may freely:
either accept them by expressly consenting to their installation and use (it being specified that the user will then have the possibility of retracting their consent at any time under the conditions and according to the methods stipulated in Article 4 hereunder),
or refuse them.
