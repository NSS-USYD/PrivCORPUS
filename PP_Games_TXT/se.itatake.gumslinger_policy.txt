How do we protect your information?
High security and trust are important values for Itatake. All of our hosting partners and third party services are striving to employ reasonable protections for the collected information that are appropriate to its sensitivity, and we do not transfer any sensitive information without reasonable protections.
Do we disclose any information to outside parties?
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our games, website, conducting our business, or servicing you as described earlier in this Privacy Policy.
For how long do we store the information?
It depends on the nature of the information collected, and the purpose of it. You can read more about the differences by viewing our licensed third parties Privacy Policies mentioned above.
If you would like to delete the private information that we have collected, please see the contact details below.
Your Consent
By using our games and our site, or by opting in to our services, you consent to our Privacy Policy. If you would like to opt out, please see the contact details below or change your privacy settings in our games.
Do we use cookies?
Yes, on our web site. Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computers hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the sites or service providers systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information.
We use cookies to compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better site experiences and tools in the future.
Changes to our Privacy Policy
If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page.
