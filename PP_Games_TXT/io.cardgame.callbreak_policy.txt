Privacy Policy for Call break Ad-Supported  Game
Thank you for your interest in our product or service ("Game"). Before using the Game you should read and understand this policy, and only use the Game if you agree to this policy. This privacy policy is accessible on the first page when you open the Game for the first time on your device, and easily accessible from the navigation menu in subsequent uses. This privacy policy is also accessible on the web page on our distribution partners. This Privacy Policy helps you understand things like:
what kind of data is collected by the App
how each kind of data is collected
why each kind of such data is collected
where the collected data is sent and stored
how you have control over the data collected from you
who and how the data is shared with
how your data is protected
Personally Identifiable Information means your personal information like name, address, phone number, email address and your identifier for purchase transactions that can be used to identify you. When using digital purchases, it may be necessary to pass on the device ID to the respective payment service provider (e.g. Google LLC) in order to be able to process payment transactions. Apart from these, there are other personally identifiable information. For example, your device may have a unique identifier associated with it, or a unique IP address might be assigned to your device when you're connected to the internet. Anonymous information includes your device's make and model, connectivity status and a log of app usage (for example, what steps you took and at what time).
1. What data is collected, and how
Device Information
When you contact us, or when your device connects to our servers through internet, your device specific information like the name of device, operating system version, model and IP address are sent to our servers.
Log File
When you use our Game, it creates a log file, including information about your device's name, operating system version. The file also contains information like when and how the Game is opened, and how you interact with the Game. The collected information is anonymous, and does not contain personal information.
Crash Reporting
When you contact us, or when the Game crashes, we send the collected log information to our servers, and our service providers.
Third-Party Services
We use third-party services in the Game to display ads, update data and get analytics information from our Game. The Game makes use of different advertising partners like Google Admob, Facebook Audience Network and Unity Ads (advertising partners) to display ads. The advertising partners may use cookies, web beacons, and other storage technologies to collect or receive information from the Game and use that information to provide measurement services and target ads. When you use our Game, the third parties receive your device information like name of your device, operating system version, model and IP address. When you connect to our Game using your Facebook or Google Play account , we receive your basic profile information (email address, if you decide to provide.)
2. Why the data is collected
We use the log data to reproduce a problem that you had in the Game. Since the advertising partners serve ads in the Game, they collect information that is needed by them to serve the purpose.
3. How the data is used
We use the information we collect from our Game to provide you with necessary services, improve, protect and maintain the Game. We also use the information to develop new features, services or products, and to protect your private information. We use the crash reports and log information to fix the issues in our Game and improve usability. We use your non-personally identifiable device information and logs to analyze the performance of our Game across different devices.
4. Where the data is sent and stored
Data collected from the game may be processed and stored in servers located in and outside of your country.
5. You have control on your data
We put you in charge of what information you want to share. You can request us by email to ask for the data we have about you. If we haven't already deleted your data after the purpose of collection is fulfilled, we will provide your data to you. Third party services offer you options to opt-out collection and use of information for ad targeting. If you decide to revoke access to your Facebook profile information, you can do that from Facebook account settings. If you want to revoke access to your Google Play account, you can do that from Google account permission settings. You can learn how to exercise such choice, and know about their privacy policies by following these helpful links:

6. Who the data is shared with
We never sell or trade your Personally Identifiable Information to anyone. We do not share your private or Personally Identifiable Information with any third party except for the following conditions:
- We get your opt-in consent to share such information with third party on your behalf.
- We need to provide our service providers, affiliates or trusted agents (for example, our email service providers or web hosting providers) with such information to process it for us, based on our instructions and in compliance with our Privacy Policy, after adopting appropriate confidentiality and security measures.
- We have a good-faith belief that sharing such information is necessary to meet any applicable legal requirement, enforce applicable Terms of Service, to investigate violation of our Terms of Service, to detect, prevent or address a security or technical issue, to protect our rights, or that of our customers or the public as required and permitted by law.
We may share non-personally identifiable information like analytics data, and crash reports, metadata and logs with our service providers, partners, advertisers and other companies or individuals that we work with. They may use those information according to their own privacy policy. If we are involved in merger, acquisition or asset sale, we will give you a notice in advance before the data is transferred or becomes subject to a different privacy policy. If we do not have any medium in file to send out notification to you, we will destroy the information collected from you before the event of merger, acquisition or asset sale.
7. Security measure
We always try to protect our infrastructure from unauthorized access to the data we store. We encrypt the network connection to or from this Game using SSL to prevent unauthorized access to the information in transit. We review the server logs and use monitoring tools to monitor our servers in a regular basis. We conduct security scans of our servers regularly. We restrict access to personal information to our contractors and agents only after they sign legally enforceable non-disclosure agreement.
8. Links to Other Sites
The Game may contain links to other sites. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Note that these external sites are not operated by us. Therefore, we strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of these websites. We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.
9. Children's Privacy
The Game does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do necessary actions.
If there are any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us using the information below. We may change our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our Game or laws. Any changes made to the Privacy Policy will be applicable after you give your explicit consent. We will notify of the changes, and the effective date at the bottom of this page.
