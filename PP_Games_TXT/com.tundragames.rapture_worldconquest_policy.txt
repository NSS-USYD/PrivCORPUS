We take your privacy very seriously and our games do not store or share any Personally Identifiable Information.
Anonymous usage information may be collected by a third-party service to help us monitor crashes and understand usage patterns in order to improve the games.
We no longer display ads in our games – so no information is collected in this respect.
When sending feedback via the game your unique device ID will be sent to us along with your email and message. This allows us to provide reimbursements to players. We do not use it for any other purpose.
If you wish us to delete any of your personal information then please get in touch at
privacy@tundragames.com
