Private Policy
This Privacy Policy describes how Appscreat Mobile Studio (hereinafter referred to as the «Company» or «we»), as the developer of related mobile applications (hereinafter referred to as the «App») available for download on the Google Play and App Store, collects information when You are using the Application. The policy also explains how we use and disclose the information we collect and explains your rights in relation to that information.
By using the Applications, you consent to the collection, storage, use and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
If you do not agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy, you may not use the Application.
The security of your personal data is important to us.
1. Age restrictions
We do not knowingly collect personal data and do not provide services to Users under the age of 18.
2. What information we collect about you
The types of data we collect and process include the following:
-Location
-Approximate location
-Exact location
-Application Details
-Bug reports
-Diagnostics
-Other data
-Identifiers
-IP address
-Unique Device Identifier (UDID)
-International Mobile Equipment Identity (IMEI)
-Device MAC address
-Advertising identifiers (Android, IOS)
Additional data may be collected if you choose to share it, for example, if you complete fields on your profile.
3. How your personal information is used
We may use your personal information for the following purposes:
-to identify you when using the Applications;
-to collect data about how users interact with the application and its performance.
-to ensure that the Apps perform optimally for you and your device;
-to display targeted ads and marketing messages or measure the effectiveness of advertising.
-to personalize the application: show recommended content or offers.
-to notify you about changes to our Apps;
-to provide you with information about updates and other special offers and new features of our Apps;
4. Where do we store your personal data
We take appropriate organizational and technical measures to protect the personal data we hold. Access to your personal data is granted only to those persons who, in our opinion, really need to get acquainted with them in the course of their work.
The information we collect from you may be transferred to and may be stored in a location outside the European Economic Area (EEA). It may also be processed by our employees, as well as employees of one of our subsidiaries or a third party engaged by us operating outside the EEA. By submitting your personal data to us, you consent to such transfer, storage or processing. We will take all reasonable steps necessary to ensure the safety of your personal data in all cases listed in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
By submitting your personal data to us, you agree that we will keep your data for as long as necessary (up to 5 years) in order to operate any of the services we provide or to comply with applicable laws, requests and relevant orders of courts of competent jurisdiction.
5. To whom your personal data may be transferred
We may share your personal data with third parties in the following cases:
-for advertising purposes;
-to manage or administer certain aspects of our Applications; for analysis purposes; to improve our Applications;
-to protect the rights, property, or safety of us or other users of the Applications;
-where we are required or permitted to do so by law;
-if we (or in fact all of our assets) are acquired by a third party, in which case the personal information we hold about our users will be one of the transferred assets.
We may share aggregated information about how our users use our Apps with third parties, but such information does not include personally identifiable information.
6. Cookie Policy
Cookies are small text files that will be installed by our Applications on your device, they allow us to provide certain features in our Application.
7. Advertisers
From time to time, we may include third party advertisers or sponsors on our Applications. In such a case, we do not disclose any information that identifies specific users, but we may provide them with aggregated information about our users.
We may also use this aggregated information to help advertisers connect with the audience they are targeting (for example, men in London).
We may use the personal information we collect from you to satisfy requests from our advertisers or sponsors to show their ads to such targeted audiences.
8. Links
Our Applications may contain links from other sites and services, as well as leading to other sites and services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of third party websites. Please check the privacy practices of such websites and services before submitting any personal information to them.
9. Rights
You have the right to ask us to stop using your personal data for marketing, analytics or other purposes. You may opt out of collecting your data by contacting us at
support@appscreat.net
10. Policy changes
We reserve the right to unilaterally change our Privacy Policy at any time.
