Kasur Dev Studio Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy applies to our games and applications which are available in the markets.
1. Sharing
We do NOT share any personally identifiable information (such as name, address, email or phone) with other companies or any external services.
2. Location Data.
We do NOT use or collect your precise geographic location.
3. We take your privacy very seriously.
We do NOT collect personally identifiable information about you. In other words, we do not collect information such as your name, address, phone number, email address etc..
Our apps may contain social integration or other social features (like Google Play Services). Some of our apps contain a link to our blog (www.kasurdev.blogspot.com). If you click the link we open your default web browser to load our website.
Some of our apps may use analytics services (like Flurry or Google Analytics). It helps us to design better user experience. Also in this case we do NOT collect any personally identifiable information.
We do NOT knowingly contact or collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly obtain parental consent or remove the information.
4. Ads
We use ads in our products. We use only banners and interstitial (full screen) ads. We do our best to make sure that ads do not interfere in the gameplay. We do NOT allow advertising companies to collect any personally identifiable data. Ad companies use device identifiers to anonymously personalise content and ads.
