Thank you for playing our games! - We want to tell you how we handle, respect and secure your privacy.
The Short Form
We, the Four Fats take user privacy and information extremely seriously, because as gamers that's what we would want too.  So, we only work with the most secure businesses that have a proven track record of data security.
Our games, relative to the games industry at large, are very limited in what we collect in terms of data from you and we stick to our philosophy of using the minimum amount to run a smooth operation and allow the player to... you know, just have a good time.
The Long Form
The Four Fats respects and upholds the confidentiality of information and the privacy of individuals and is committed to user privacy. Because we gather important information from our users, we have established this Privacy Policy as a means to communicate our information gathering and dissemination practices. By accessing this Website and/or using our applications, you acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy and also agree to the Terms of Use of the Website located at
http://www.fourfats.com/privacy
​
What We Collect
You have a number of options regarding the information you give us. You may give us your your name and/or e-mail address. You may use these to log into our applications. In conclusion, we collection information on those using our applications, but it is done to improve our services, and in a strictly anonymous form.
Children under the age of 13 are not permitted to register at our site or software, and we are not allowed to collect any information regarding their personal matters. We advise that children between the ages of 13-18 ask their parents or legal guardian for permission before signing on.
We use our information that we collect to improve our services, and to design games and apps that you would like to view. We also may send you emails (opt-in) regarding our services, but you can unsubscribe at any time. If required by law or a subpoena, we will have to disclose your personal information, we have no say regarding this matter. We also use technical information to help our service. We may share information with our partners to correct problems or to enhance our services. They may see the information that you provide, but you cannot opt out of this.
We may use third party advertising companies, however we do not disclose your personal information to these companies. The Four Fats takes your personal information very seriously, it is located in a secure server. If you have any other questions, please contact us at info@fourfats.com
The Ad Networks We Use
As part of some of our games, we may use the services from one or more ad networks.  We take huge precaution in only choosing those with the best user experience, security and flexibility for the user.

How We Manage Your Personal Information
You may have contact with us personally, by telephone, mail, over the Internet or other electronic medium. We take all reasonable steps to keep your Personal Information secure in a combination of secure computer storage, hard copy files and other records. We take steps to protect the personal information we hold from misuse, loss, unauthorized access, modification or disclosure. You have the right to access the Personal Information you have provided to us. If you require details of your Personal Information held by us, we will need to verify your identity before meeting your request, which we will process in a reasonable time. If you find that the information we hold about you is inaccurate or out of date then we will promptly correct it upon receipt of the changes from you.
Personal Information Provided By You
The type of Personal Information that may be collected or submitted to the Four Fats includes, but is not limited to the following:
Your player name
Contact info (Name & E-mail address)
Any further data that you provide to us voluntarily (i.e. if you e-mail us for help restoring data)
Personal Information Automatically Collected
Your default player name and assigned player ID number
Your device operating system, device model and language setting
Game data regarding game progression
Why Do We Collect Personal Information
The Four Fats offers various products and services to our players and also strives to meet the needs of our players. We use your Personal Information for internal purposes such as tracking your order, analyzing your preferences, and noting trends and statistics. To do this effectively, we need to collect certain Personal Information.
How Can I Opt Out On My Device?
As of the effective date of this policy, Apple and Google offered the owners of devices that use their operating systems (iOS and Android) with options for limited tracking and targeting in relation to ads. We do not control your device-level opt-out processes and are not responsible for any choices you make through such a mechanism or for the continued availability, accuracy, effectiveness, or location of that mechanism. Using a device-level opt-out mechanism may opt you out of all third-party personalized advertisements, not just those delivered by only one ad network.

Children’s Privacy
Our Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at
info@fourfats.com
so that we will be able to do necessary actions.
Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately, after they are posted on this page.
