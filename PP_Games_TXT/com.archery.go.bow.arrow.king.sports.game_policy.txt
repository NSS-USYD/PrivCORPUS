Privacy Policy
Welcome! This Privacy Policy explains how "Archery Go" or "we" collects, uses, and shares your personal information while you play Archery Go games ("Games").
We may make changes to this Privacy Policy from time to time.This Policy applies to people who use the Games.
THE DATA WE COLLECT
Data you provide us.
- Contact information ( email address)
- Player name
Data we collect automatically.
- Precise geo-location data
- Data about your account and game progress
- Your IP address and mobile device identifiers (such as your device ID, advertising ID, MAC address, IMEI)
- Data about your device, such as device name and operating system, browser type and language
Data about your use of the Service, such as gameplay data and your interactions with other players inside the Service
Data we collect from our partners.
- Data we receive if you link a third party tool with the Service (such as Google)
- Data to fight fraud (such as refund abuse in games or click fraud in advertising)
- Data for advertising and analytics purposes, so we can provide you a better Service
WHY DO WE COLLECT YOUR DATA
For optimal performance of Services for you and your device.
- Create accounts and allow you to play our games and use our Service
- Verify and confirm payments
- Provide and deliver products and services you request
To make the Service more suitable for our players.
- Update and develop player profiles
- Develop and improve the Service and player experience
- Enable you to communicate with other players
To show personalized advertisements.
To keep the Service safe.
In order to keep the Service and its social features safe and fair, to fight fraud.
WHO CAN SEE YOUR DATA
Other players and users may, for example, see your profile data, in-game activities and read the messages you have posted.
Security.
Archery Go has implemented technology and policies to safeguard your privacy from unauthorised access and improper use
Data retention.
We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you the Service. We will for example periodically de-identify unused game accounts and we regularly review and de-identify unnecessary data.
YOUR RIGHTS
You have the right to send us a request to cease using your personal data for marketing, analytical or other purposes.
Policy towards children
Archery Go Games are intended for anyone who is legally entitled to own a device that access them and play them and anyone who is legally entitled to make in-app purchases by the payment functionality the Games provide. However, Archery Go does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information in relation to children under the age of 13.
