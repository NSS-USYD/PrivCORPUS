COOKING MASTER
This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") applies to "COOKING MASTER" game (com.cooking.games.fever.girls.city.craze.chef.master.dream.family) , the Android game developed by my team. The policy explains which information to be treated by the app and usages of them.
***PERMISSION INFORMATION***
The app does not collect nor send any personal identifiable data. In other words we never treat information such as your name, phone number, e-mail address, address or geographic location.
The app captures audio from output mix and mic. We use them only for the recording function and audio visualization and never use them for any other purposes.
Details of requested permissions:
* RECORD_AUDIO
- To capture audio data from mic for recording function.
- The audio data do not send anywhere from your devices.
* MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS
- Set audio file as ringtone.
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
- To read audio files from storage.
- To read album art picture files from storage.
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
- To save effect edited files.
- To save recording files.
WAKE_LOCK
- To provide stable audio playback.
INTERNET
- To show ads.
