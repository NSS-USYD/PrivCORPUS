Privacy Policy
玩猴遊戲股份有限公司(下稱「我們」或「玩猴」) 尊重並承諾保護您的個人隱私，本隱私權政策將幫助您了解我們如何蒐集、使用及保護我們自用戶所取得的個人資料。
其中「遊戲」或「應用程式」是指我們創建的遊戲，包括各種版本和跨所有設備和平台的延伸內容；產品則指包含「遊戲」、「應用程式」、「網頁」或「線上服務」等由我們所開發及提供的各種功能及服務。
Macaca Games Inc. (hereinafter referred to as “Macaca Games”, “we/our/us”) are committed to protecting and respecting your
						privacy. This policy covers how we treat the information we collect and receive from users of our games (“users” or
						“you”).
The terms “game” or “app” refer to the games we create, including its various editions and expansion content across all devices
						and platforms.
我們蒐集的資料
Information We Collect
概述
Generally
我們僅收集本隱私政策中特別描述的訊息。 我們不會於遊戲中向用戶索取或收集任何個人信息（除了您聯絡時主動提供的電子郵件地址，以便我們可以就您提出的問題進行解答與回覆）。
We collect only the information specifically described in this Privacy Policy. We do not ask for or collect any personal
						information from our users in order to use the game (with the exception of allowing you to provide an email address
						so that we can contact you regarding a support request that you initiate). This includes not collecting personal information
						from children.
取得支援
Support Requests
如果您需要支援協助，您可以向我們提供電子郵件地址。 如果您在這種情況下向我們提供電子郵件地址，我們只會用它來就您的支持請求與您聯繫，並將根據您的請求將其從我們的系統中刪除。
You may have the option of providing us with an email address in the event you initiate a support request. If you provide
						us with an email address in this situation, we will only use it to contact you with regard to your support request
						and will remove it from our system at your request.

In some of our product will provide subscribe service, you may providing us with an email address.If you provide us with
						an email address in this situation, we will only use it to send news of our product t and will remove it from our system
						at your request.
