PRIVACY POLICY
This policy describes what information we collect and how it is used and shared.
What kinds of information do we collect?
- Information you provide: we collect the content and other information you provide. Such as birthday, location.
- Information about payments: we collect information about the purchase or transaction.
How do we use this information?
- Provide, improve and support function in game: We use your information you provide to make the experience in the game better, Such as Location is use to find giftbox or pet around you, camera use when you catch pet with AR function, audio record use when you want talk with other player ingame, photo library to save screenshot you take in game.
- Promote safety and security: We use the information we have to help verify accounts and activity, and to promote safety and security. We work hard to protect your account.  Information about the purchase or transaction is collect to checking your purchase if you have the problem when you buy Inapp in game.
How is this information shared?
- We only share some information you provide with other player in game such as your zodiac ( Not your birthday), city where you live ( Not exactly your location), avatar you upload. We don’t share any things else.
How can I manage or delete information about me?
- You can manage the content and information you share by open your profile in game.
- We store data for as long as it is necessary to provide products and services to you and others, including those described above. Information associated with your account will be kept until your account is deleted, unless we no longer need the data to provide products and services.
- You can delete your account any time. When you delete your account, we delete all data and information on this account.
