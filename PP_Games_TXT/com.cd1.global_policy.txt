Privacy Policy for the YouTube Data API
The YouTube Data API is a service provided by YouTube that allows developers to retrieve and display YouTube content and data on their own websites or applications. This privacy policy document outlines how YouTube collects, uses, and discloses information when developers use the YouTube Data API.
Collection of Information
When a developer uses the YouTube Data API, YouTube may collect information about the developer and their users, including:
Information about the developer's YouTube account, such as the developer's name, email address, and profile picture.
Information about the developer's YouTube channel(s), such as the channel ID, title, and description.
Information about the developer's users, such as their YouTube channel IDs, video IDs, and search queries.
Use of Information
YouTube may use the information it collects to provide the YouTube Data API service, to improve the service, and to personalize the user experience on YouTube and other Google services.
YouTube may also use the information to create aggregate statistics and analytics about the use of the YouTube Data API, and to detect and prevent fraud or abuse of the service.
Disclosure of Information
YouTube may disclose the information it collects to third-party service providers who help provide the YouTube Data API service, such as hosting providers and content delivery networks. YouTube may also disclose the information to law enforcement or other government authorities if required by law.
YouTube may also share the information with its affiliates, including other companies within the Google group of companies, for the purposes described in this privacy policy.
Security
YouTube takes reasonable measures to protect the information it collects from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure. However, no security measures are perfect, and YouTube cannot guarantee the security of the information.
Your Rights
Developers and their users have certain rights with respect to the information that YouTube collects through the YouTube Data API. These include the right to access, correct, and delete the information. Developers and their users may also opt-out of certain uses of the information, such as personalized advertising.
Changes to this Privacy Policy
YouTube may update this privacy policy from time to time. If YouTube makes significant changes, YouTube will provide notice by posting a prominent notice on the YouTube website or by sending an email to the developer's account.
Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact YouTube using the contact information provided on the YouTube website.
