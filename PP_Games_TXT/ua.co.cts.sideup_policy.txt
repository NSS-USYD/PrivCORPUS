Privacy Policy Move the Box
Google Play requires developers to provide a valid privacy policy when the app requests or handles sensitive user or device information. We want to outline the way we handle information we receive about users in
Move the Box
app.
Versions of Move the Box game currently available on the market only collect and store information obtained on the client device through third-party libraries. Our own code doesn't collect and/or store any information from the user outside of user's device.
Third party libraries included in Move the Box that collect and/or store user information are Google Analytics and Millennial Media ad serving solution.
Google Analytics is a service that provides some aggregate information about users of our application. One can find privacy policy in place for Google services at
https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/
Millennial Media is a solution we use to serve in-game ads. Sensitive permissions required by some version of our app (
READ_PHONE_STATE) were introduced due to requirements of this service.
Millennial Media company was acquired by AOL, and one can find the privacy policy in place for their services at
http://privacy.aol.com/privacy-policy/
If you believe this information is not full or you have any additional questions please contact us at
