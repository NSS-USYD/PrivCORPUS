Privacy matters, but it can be confusing. This page explains our approach to privacy on all our products and how it affects you.
Vanilla B.V. ("Vanilla") collects some personal information for most of our products. For our applications, we use device identifiers to personalise content and ads, and to analyse our traffic. We also share such identifiers and other information from your device with our advertising and analytics partners.

For our websites, we use cookies to personalise content and ads. We also share information about your use of our sites with our social media, advertising, and analytics partners.
