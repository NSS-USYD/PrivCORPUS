Privacy Policy
GamesGang Studios
has adopted this privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) to explain how GamesGang Studios collects, stores, and uses the information collected in connection with GamesGang Studio’s Services.
BY INSTALLING, USING, REGISTERING TO OR OTHERWISE ACCESSING THE SERVICES, YOU AGREE TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY AND GIVE AN EXPLICIT AND INFORMED CONSENT TO THE PROCESSING OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA IN ACCORDANCE WITH THIS PRIVACY POLICY. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY, PLEASE DO NOT INSTALL, USE, REGISTER TO OR OTHERWISE ACCESS THE SERVICES. GamesGang Studios reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at reasonable times, so please review it frequently. If GamesGang Studios makes material or significant changes to this Privacy Policy, GamesGang Studios may post a notice on GamesGang Studio’s website along with the updated Privacy Policy. Your continued use of Services will signify your acceptance of the changes to this Privacy Policy.
Non-personal data
For purposes of this Privacy Policy, “non-personal data” means information that does not directly identify you. The types of non-personal data GamesGang Studios may collect and use include, but are not limited to: application properties, including, but not limited to application name, package name and icon installed on your device. Your checkin (include like, recommendation) of a game will be disclosed to all GamesGang Studios users.
GamesGang Studios may use and disclose to GamesGang Studios’s partners and contractors the collected non-personal data for purposes of analyzing usage of the Services, advertisement serving, managing and providing the Services and to further develop the Services and other GamesGang Studios services and products.
You recognize and agree that the analytics companies utilized by GamesGang Studios may combine the information collected with other information they have independently collected from other services or products relating to your activities. These companies collect and use information under their own privacy policies.
Personal Data
Please note that certain features of the Services may be able to connect to your social networking sites to obtain additional information about you. In such cases, GamesGang Studios may be able to collect certain information from your social networking profile when your social networking site permits it, and when you consent to allow your social networking site to make that information available to GamesGang Studios. This information may include, but is not limited to, your name, profile picture, gender, user ID, email address, your country, your language, your time zone, the organizations and links on your profile page, the names and profile pictures of your social networking site “friends” and other information you have included in your social networking site profile. GamesGang Studios may associate and/or combine as well as use information collected by GamesGang Studios and/or obtained through such social networking sites in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
Disclosure and Transfer of Personal Data
GamesGang Studios collects and processes personal data on a voluntary basis and it is not in the business of selling your personal data to third parties. Personal data may, however, occasionally be disclosed in accordance with applicable legislation and this Privacy Policy. Additionally,  GamesGang Studios may disclose personal data to its parent companies and its subsidiaries in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
Safeguards
GamesGang Studios follows generally accepted industry standards and maintains reasonable safeguards to attempt to ensure the security, integrity and privacy of the information in GamesGang Studios’s possession. Only those persons with a need to process your personal data in connection with the fulfillment of their tasks in accordance with the purposes of this Privacy Policy and for the purposes of performing technical maintenance, have access to your personal data in GamesGang Studios’s possession. Personal data collected by GamesGang Studios is stored in secure operating environments that are not available to the public. To prevent unauthorized on-line access to personal data, GamesGang Studios maintains personal data behind a firewall-protected server. However, no system can be 100% secure and there is the possibility that despite GamesGang Studios’s reasonable efforts, there could be unauthorized access to your personal data. By using the Services, you assume this risk.
Other
Please be aware of the open nature of certain social networking and other open features of the Services GamesGang Studios may make available to you. You may choose to disclose data about yourself in the course of contributing user generated content to the Services. Any data that you disclose in any of these forums, blogs, chats or the like is public information, and there is no expectation of privacy or confidentiality. GamesGang Studios is not responsible for any personal data you choose to make public in any of these forums.
If you are under 15 years of age or a minor in your country of residence, please ask your legal guardian’s permission to use or access the Services. GamesGang Studios takes children’s privacy seriously, and encourages parents and/or guardians to play an active role in their children’s online experience at all times. GamesGang Studios does not knowingly collect any personal information from children below the aforementioned age and if GamesGang Studios learns that GamesGang Studios has inadvertently gathered personal data from children under the aforementioned age, GamesGang Studios will take reasonable measures to promptly erase such personal data from GamesGang Studios’s records.
GamesGang Studios may store and/or transfer your personal data to its affiliates and partners in and outside of EU/EEA member states and the United States in accordance with mandatory legislation and this Privacy Policy. GamesGang Studios may disclose your personal data to third parties in connection with a corporate merger, consolidation, restructuring, the sale of substantially all of GamesGang Studios’s stock and/or assets or other corporate change, including, without limitation, during the course of any due diligence process provided, however, that this Privacy Policy shall continue to govern such personal data.
GamesGang Studios regularly reviews its compliance with this Privacy Policy. If GamesGang Studios receives a formal written complaint from you, it is GamesGang Studios’s policy to attempt to contact you directly to address any of your concerns. GamesGang Studios will cooperate with the appropriate governmental authorities, including data protection authorities, to resolve any complaints regarding the collection, use, transfer or disclosure of personal data that cannot be amicably resolved between you and GamesGang Studios.
