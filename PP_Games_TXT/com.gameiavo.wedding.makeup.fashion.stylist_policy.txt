GameiAvo, its subsidiaries and its affiliates (“Company”,
“we” or “GameiAvo”) respect the privacy of the users of their mobile
applications (“User” or “you”) and are committed to protecting the User’s

the information we may collect and use when you use our Apps (as such term is
defined below).

Your consent (important, please read carefully!)
By installing the apps on your mobile device, entering into,
connecting to, accessing, and/or using the apps, you agree to the terms and
conditions set forth in this privacy policy, including the possible collection
and processing of your personal information. Please note: If you or, as
applicable, your legal guardian, disagree to any term provided herein, you may
not install, access, and/or use the apps and you are requested to promptly
erase them, and any part thereof, from your mobile device.
Please Note: If you or, as applicable, your legal guardian,
disagree to any term provided herein, you may not install, access, and/or use
the apps and you are requested to promptly erase them, and any part thereof,
From your mobile device.
If you are under the age of eighteen (18) you hereby
represent that your legal guardian has reviewed and agreed to this Privacy
Policy.
* Cookies
Where necessary, our apps use cookies to store information
about a visitor’s preferences and history in order to better serve the visitor
and/or present the visitor with customized content. Advertising partners and
other third parties may also use cookies, scripts, and/or web beacons to track
visitors to our site in order to display advertisements and other useful
information. Such tracking is done directly by the third parties through their
own servers and is subject to their own privacy policies.
* COPPA
We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act.
We do not knowingly collect personal information on children under the age of
13. When a user identifies himself or herself as a child under the age of 13
through a support request or through any feedback, we will not collect, store
or use, and will delete in a secure manner, any personal information of such
user.

Welcome to GameiAvo. We are a team of designers,
developers, and artists building various applications. We have mobile &
tablet applications developed (the applications with the site given through the
Apps). Some of the features of the services regard additional guidelines, rules,
or terms which will be posted in the apps in connection with such features. All
such terms, guidelines, and rules are included by reference in this agreement.
The applications are developed for use by all from small to
aged. We know your concern for the privacy of children and do not collect any
information about children through the Apps. Verily, apart from parent
information, we do not transmit any data saved on the applications back to
servers. “Parent Information” means a parent’s email address received with the
intention of transmitting adult information about the Apps to the parent.
Detailed view regarding the use of collected information is fully mentioned in
our Privacy Policy below.
Teenagers Privacy Policy is designated to provide you, the
legal guardian of youngsters above 18 years old with guidance regarding our
privacy policies and principles we request you to guide your children. Before
playing any games such as shooting, killing or horror, show it to your parents
first and play under the observation of parents. Play games just for enjoyment
and not for any impact negatively.

* Offline Use
GameiAvo allows offline use (without an Internet
connection and without GPS enablement) of some features of its Kids Apps.
Offline use of Kids Apps will preclude the collection by GameiAvo and/or
Third Parties of any information pertaining to you, as the legal guardian of a
Kid, and/or your use (including, as applicable, information pertaining to your
Kid and his/her use) of the Kids Apps.
* Online Use
There are three types of data and information that may be
collected from Users of our Kids Apps:
* Non-personal Information:
GameiAvo allows Third Parties to collect
non-identifiable and anonymous information as detailed below (“Kids
Non-personal Information”). To put it simply, with respect to such Kids
Non-personal Information GameiAvo and the Third Parties have no idea
of the identity of the User from whom Third Parties have collected the Kids
Non-personal Information. Kids Non-personal Information collected by Third
Parties may include the following technical information:
* Type of operation system (e.g. Android, iOS, etc.)
* Type of browser (e.g. Explorer, Firefox, Chrome, Safari, etc.)
* Screen resolution (e.g. 800×600, 1024×768, etc.)
* Language (e.g. English)
* Device type (e.g. Galaxy, HTC, iPhone, etc.)
* Geo-Location – only general location may be collected. No
street name or city/town name is collected.
* The purpose for such collection:
GameiAvo allows Third Parties collection of
Non-personal Information for strict and limited purposes. Namely, for the
limited purpose of analysing the Kids Apps in order to provide the Users of
Kids Apps with the Kids Apps functionalities and performance.
* Kids’ Persistent Device Identifier and No Other Kids
Personal Identifiable Information:
Third Parties may also collect a persistent device
identifier from Kids, such as IP address unique device identifier, advertising
identifier (when available on a device) etc. (“Kids’ Persistent Device
Identifier”). GameiAvo does not collect and does not allow Third
Parties to collect from Kids any personal information that may identify an
individual (other than the Kids’ Persistent Device Identifier which is
collected for the limited purpose of internal operations of the Apps) and any
other personally identifiable information of a private and/or sensitive nature
about the Kids. We note that GameiAvo may collect, use and provide
access to the User’s advertising identifier in accordance with the applicable
platform provider’s policies.
GameiAvo does not allow any additional personally
identifiable information to be linked to the Kids’ Persistent Device
Identifier, even from other services.
