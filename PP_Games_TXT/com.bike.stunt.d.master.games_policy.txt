GAMING STARS INC.
PRIVACY POLICY ABOUT SERVICES of GAMING STARS INC
Privacy Policy about our services:
Your privacy is important to
Gaming Stars Inc
therefore we never use any information or data without your permission.
The Permissions which we use and how we use it:
1- Camera
2- Location
3- Storage
4- Internet State
5- Network State
6- Billing
1- Camera:
Some times games on
Gaming Stars Inc
need camera permission
for better user experience in the game. If you don't like to permit the game to access camera you can deny it, but in this way you will not be able to use a feature of the that game.
2- Location:
Some games on
Gaming Stars Inc
can request you to permit this game to access your
location. The game will never use this permission. In fact I am using 3rd parties APIs as Admob or other advertising party. These parties use this permission to provide you better results and show you the ads according to your local trends.
3- Storage
If our game is using storage permission, it is to store necessary data of the game in your device. You can get this data from your device after to uninstall the game.
4- Internet State
Internet State is just for better performance of the game so that according to those states our game may use minimum or maximum internet while playing the game.
5- Network State
The
Network State
is an indicator found on a variety of 
modern smartphones that shows whether these devices are 
connected to a mobile network or wifi internet. As a basic indicator, the mobile network state simply shows 
whether that device is currently using a connection to the particular 
carrier network. This permission helps the game while accessing to the internet in online or multi players games.
6- Billing
Billing permission is very simple and can be used in those games which have some purchasable features.
How we protect your information:
We have implemented commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures in games developing to protect your personal information from accidental loss and from loss, misuse and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and destruction.
However, please note that although we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no app, website, Internet transmission, computer system or wireless connection is completely secure.
Privacy for children
a
Changes to the Privacy Policy
Gaming Stars Inc
may, in its sole discretion, change this Privacy Policy from time to time, any and all changes will be reflected here and the date new versions are posted will be stated at the top of this Privacy Policy, so please review it periodically.
b
We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
Contact Us:
If you have any question about our Privacy Police described above, you can contact us and if you need some more information you can send us Email at:
gaming.stars.inc@gmail.com
