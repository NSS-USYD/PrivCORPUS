Privacy Policy
At Velvet Void Games, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and thus only use data when absolutely required. For this reason, our game Chicken Odyssey does not collect any personal data from its users at all. As a result, we do not request, track, use, or share any personal information from our users.
In the case of revisions to our policy, we will update this document accordingly. If you have any questions, you may reach us at velvetvoid at gmail.com.
