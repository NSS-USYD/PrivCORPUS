The use of analytics services also helps us to improve the game for you, to find crashes and issues in the game, and to improve the performance of our websites/apps. For this purpose, we process crash information, ANR information (the situation if the game freezes), and device information (battery, Wifi situation, CPU, memory). New user acquisition, remarketing and campaign tracking
The following processes help us to find new users, remarket existing users and measure the effectiveness of our advertisements:
New user acquisition: In order to acquire new users, we create advertisements (banners/videos) for Governor of Poker 3 and provide our advertisements to third-party user acquisition companies which show our ads in their apps or on their websites. If you click on the banner/video shown to you in a third-party app or on a third-party website, you will be redirected to the app stores (Google, Steam, Microsoft, Samsung Galaxy, Huawei, Apple or web version of Governor of Poker 3) where you can install and/or play Governor of Poker 3. The information that you have clicked/viewed the advertisement (campaign information) will be sent from the user acquisition companies to an attribution platform provider (Appsflyer) and will then be passed by Appsflyer to the app store. If you install Governor of Poker 3, the campaign information will also be passed from Appflyer to us and will be processed to measure the effectiveness of our advertisements. The described data processing activities help us prevent fraud, to track and optimize our campaigns, and to reward our user acquisition partners. This is based on consent obtained from you by the third-party app or the third-party website.
When you start playing Governor of Poker 3, we collect and share post install events (for example, the level you reached within the game and purchase segments) either through the Appsflyer SDK or directly with the user acquisition companies which helps them to optimize their services. Post install events also help us to optimize our Services. We share these post install events based on our legitimate interest.
Furthermore, we use FacebookГўв‚¬в„ўs Гўв‚¬ВќLookalike AudienceГўв‚¬Вќ feature to find new users based on the user segments we have created for our user base. These similar users will be shown advertisements (banners/videos) for Governor of Poker 3 to encourage them to click the advertisements and install Governor of Poker 3 through the app- store.
Remarketing: We also provide our advertisements to third-party user acquisition companies to show advertisements for Governor of Poker 3 to existing users and to encourage them to come back to the game. For this purpose, if you are a registered user and if you have provided the relevant consent, we will share your user ID, your country, your device identifiers and language information with third-party user acquisition companies. This processing is based on our legitimate interest.
