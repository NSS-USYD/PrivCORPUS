Privacy Policy
Kool Games gives highest level of importance to the privacy of your personal information.
Kool Games will strictly comply with all relevant legislative requirements. The legislative requirements will override the provisions of this policy in the event of any inconsistency.
Kool Games may amend this Privacy Policy at any time and the updated policy shall be available to you through the same link, such amendments will take effect from the date of publishing on our website.
If you are a minor at law, you must get your parent’s or guardian’s permission before providing us with any personal information.
What information we may collect?
For better user experience, Kool Games may collect the following information:
Your demographic information such as location, preferences and interests.
Your public information if social media is used in the app.
Your game-play name (This is not required to be your real name. Kool Games uses this name to address you during the game-play)
We may collect information regarding your use of our games, including information about the duration of your use.
We may also collect information regarding the device you use to access our games, including (where applicable) its model, operating system, screen resolution and other technical information.
Why we may collect your information?
Kool Games may collect your information to understand your needs and provide you with a better service, and in particular for the following reasons:
Internal record keeping.
Non-Personal Information may be used for:
statistical and research purposes
customization and improvement of our games.
Personal Information may be used to reply you when you contact through support channels (Facebook/ Email).
Personal Information like Location/ IP may be used to show you Ads according to your location, this is called contextual advertisement.
How do we collect your information?
Kool Games may collect your information in following scenarios:
When you will use our games.
When you contact us through Email/Facebook and/or other Support Channels.
Use and Disclosure of your information:
Kool Games will not use or disclose personal information about you other than:
For the purpose of providing or offering goods and services to you.
For any other purpose that you may reasonably expect.
For any other purpose authorized by law.
For any other purpose disclosed to or authorized by you. This may include disclosures to organizations that provide us with professional advice, such as solicitors, accountants and business advisors.
We may provide aggregated or anonymous information to third parties for the purposes of developing and delivering targeted advertising. We will not share your personal information with such third parties without your consent.

To opt-out please stop using our game or do the following:
