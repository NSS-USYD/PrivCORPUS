Privacy Policy for Hindu Calendar
-----------------------------------
The app Hindu Calendar uses Google Analytics (Firebase Analytics).
It tracks how the app is being used. It does not track or use any personal information.
It will accumulate usage data and transmit it over the internet.
For instance, OS version, feature of the app being used.
The data will be used to make the User Interface and Experience better.
The app uses third party services that may collect information used to identify you.
Link to privacy policy of third party service providers used by the app
Google Play Services and Google Admob :
https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/
Firebase Analytics :
https://firebase.google.com/policies/analytics
MoPub :
https://www.mopub.com/legal/privacy/
