Connect and disconnect from Wi-Fi:
This permission is used in Keyboard Style application setting and notification toolbar in order to connect and disconnect from Wi-Fi.

This permission is allows Keyboard Style  applications to make the phone vibrate once after users set the device on vibrate, confirming the setting has been setting on.
