Information we collect:
Location
Based on the information on network availability and your use, we determine the approximate position of your mobile phone. If you activate location services on your devices, your operating system supports it and you give the app permission to do so, we also collect information about your GPS coordinates or your radio cell. If you give the app permission to do so, we will collect your location even if you are not actively using the MyVodafone app. In this way, we can see where we can still improve the network. And you get better help with network problems.
For this purpose, we store:
The cellular cell to which your device is connected to.
The BSSID of the active WLAN
The GPS position data with speed and direction information only for a speed test. Or if other apps, e.g. a navigation app, request the data.
how long you have turned on your device.
how strongly you receive the signal.
how long you call or surf, with date and time.
how the call or connection ended.
an hourly connection test to the Internet
Download, upload and response speeds
how long it takes to establish an Internet connection.
the connection quality to the WLAN
the MAC addresses of the Wi-Fi hotspots your device detects.
the amount of data consumed per hour
the amount of data consumed in standby mode
the proportion of data in the WLAN and in the mobile network
Information We Don't Collect by MyVodafone App:
We do not collect information about:
Your number, hence no tracking is possible
The numbers you are calling. Or from whom you are called.
The senders or recipients of your SMS messages, emails or Internet chats
Your conversation or message content or browsing history
The content your apps send, receive, or otherwise process.
