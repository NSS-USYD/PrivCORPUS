What information does the App collect, and what does 3M do with it?
The App collects the following types of information:
Technical data. Technical data, such as information about your usage of the App (e.g., features used, content viewed, dates and times of interactions) and technical data about your device, such as operating system, model, etc. The App collects this technical data automatically when you download the App and as you use it. We use this data to evaluate the usefulness of App features to users and to make improvements to the App.
Location information. Location information, meaning precise real-time location information collected using your device's GPS technology. The App uses location information to provide you with a record of addresses of locations where you captured physical Post-it® Notes with the App. This address information is displayed in the App, and included in export formats such as PowerPoint® and PDF. Please note that we will NOT access precise location information from your device unless you grant the App permission to do so; if you do not grant the App permission to access precise location information, your captures will not be stored with address information and no address information will be added to exported formats.
Images. The App provides an optional Handwriting Recognition (“OCR”) feature to recognize and transcribe handwriting on your notes. If you use the OCR feature, then the App will send copies of images you select to be transcribed to a third party service provider to be converted to digital text, after which the third party service provider will return the transcribed text to your device and then discard the copies of your notes. The transcribed text is stored with your notes in the App. Neither 3M nor the third-party provider collects any information about the contents or text of your notes.
Shared notes. The App includes an optional Brainstorming Session feature where you can opt to share selected notes with the host of a remote workshop, such as a brainstorm. If you use the Brainstorming Session feature, i.e. participate in a session, the notes that you select will be temporarily uploaded to the Post-it® Cloud servers so that the session host may download them. Participants enter a display name to connect to a session. The display name is associated with notes that participants choose to share with the host. Shared notes are deleted from the Post-it® Cloud servers and from participants’ devices at the conclusion of the session and are not retained afterwards. After deletion, shared notes are only stored locally on the host’s device.

Other Tracking Technologies
We use analytics providers that through technologies such as software development kits, device identifiers, and statistical IDs, are able to capture and remember certain information related to your use of the App. We use these tracking technologies to help us understand our users’ usage patterns on the App so that we can tailor new product features for our users’ benefit to offer better App experiences and tools in the future.
This functionality may be disabled by opting out. To opt out, perform the following steps inside the app on the device where you wish to opt out.
Go to the “Settings” view in the app.
Pick “About the App” and then “Analytics Data”.
Deselect “Share information with Post-it®”.
The same screen also allows you as a user to view and delete the information that has been gathered by our analytics providers.
