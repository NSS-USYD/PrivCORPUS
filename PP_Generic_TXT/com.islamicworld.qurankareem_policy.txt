Quran Karim – Privacy Policy
April 20, 2017
May 8, 2017
/
NimdA
This privacy policy governs your use of the software application
Quran Kareem
for mobile devices that was created by Islamic World. The Application is an Islamic application to start reading Quran and listen its Recitation with translations.
The application obtains the following information from your device and given along are the reasons:
Automatically Collected Information
Network Access & Internet Permission
– The permission for Network Access & Internet is required in order to enable ad serving on the application.
Device ID & call information
Read phone status and identity – This permission is required to ensure the application starts from the same state if you receive a call while using app.
