Flashlight by Szymon Dyja - Privacy policy
Application is simple android application that turns mobile device into a flashlight.
Application features:
2 light sources (if available)
2 bright modes: always ON or strobe/blinking/flashing with frequency to choose from
8 colors screen to choose from
Auto Light ON when you launch app
Clear and modern design
Widget - turn on/off light from home screen
It works on the locked screen
What information we collect?
Application does not collect personal information like name, surname, age, place of birth.
