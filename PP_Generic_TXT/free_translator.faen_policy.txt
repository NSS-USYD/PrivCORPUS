Klays Development Privacy policy
What personal information do we collect from the people that use our app?
Klays Development does not ask for any personal or sensitive user data. Klays Development will never use the following: personally identifiable information, financial and payment information, authentication information, phonebook or contact data, microphone and camera sensor data, and sensitive device data.
When do we collect information?
Klays Development never collects any information from the user.
Third-party disclosure
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties any information of the user.
Third-party links
We do not include or offer third-party products or services in our application.
