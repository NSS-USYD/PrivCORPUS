This page is used to inform website visitors regarding my policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use my Service.
Respecting your privacy is a fundamental policy of albert, and therefore, we formulate this Privacy Policy to inform you of how we collect, use and share your personal information.
Non- Personal Information
We may collect and use non-personal information in the following circumstances. To have a better understanding in user’s behavior, solve problems in products and services, improve our products, services and advertising, we may collect non-personal information such as installed application name and package name, the data of install, frequency of use, country, equipment and channel.
Permissions uses in App and its uses
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE:
This permission used for store or save your created video on SD card or memory card. It also required for store temporary file for animations.
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE:
This permission used for show all photos on our app. Photos are use for creating video.
INTERNET:
This permission required to download video themes or music. It also required showing ads on app.
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE:
This permission required to check internet is available or not before downloading video effect theme or music.
