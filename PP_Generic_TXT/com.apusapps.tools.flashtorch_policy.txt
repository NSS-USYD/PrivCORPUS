Without cookie, the userГўв‚¬в„ўs experience of Internet will be greatly reduced, and some website features or services may not even work properly. Websites may use cookie to recommend advertisements. For example, some websites use cookie to record websites that you click on to prevent you from seeing duplicate advertisements.
