aCalendar is an Android app developed by
Tapir Apps GmbH

aCalendar is a user interfaces to the Android calendar storage for events and Android contacts for birthdays.
The plus version includes all features that can be bought as in-app-purchase in the regular (free) version.
This privacy policy governs both app versions.
For any questions regarding our privacy policy please contact our support by email to
support@tapirapps.de

Â§ Calendar and Contacts data
aCalendar operates on Android's local calendar and contacts data.
aCalendar never transfers your calendar or contacts data to Tapir Apps or third parties.
Please be aware, that calendar and contacts data can be read by any app on your device with the appropriate permissions, as these are standard Android interfaces.
If you use cloud calendars to sync your calendar data, please read the privacy policies of your cloud services.
