Load Shedding Notifier privacy policy
When you use apps, you trust the developer with your information. This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what
		data is collected by abisoft, why we collect it, and what we do with it. This is important; we hope you will take time
		to read it carefully.
Personal information
Personal information includes your name, address, telephone number, email address, credit card information, and similar data.
		Load Shedding Notifier optionally records anonymous location data, in order to assist users with determining the best area to use based on
		their current location. This data is in no way associated with you as a person. You need to enable location data before we can record it.
		You can switch this off at any time from the privacy settings in the app.
Device information
Device information includes your hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information,
		and similar data. Load Shedding Notifier stores anonymous device information on a third party server in order to send push notifications to
		your device.
Log information
Load Shedding Notifier does not automatically capture logs from the app. However, in the event of an app crash, you may be prompted to send
		the app logs to Abisoft or a third party. These logs include information about the state of the app at the time of the crash
		and may be used by Abisoft to improve the stability of the app.
