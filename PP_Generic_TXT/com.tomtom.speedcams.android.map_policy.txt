TomTom AmiGO Privacy Policy
TomTom International B.V. and its affiliates around the world (‘TomTom’) believe that we can only use your data when we respect your privacy. We do everything it takes to keep your private data truly private. You don’t want to find out that other companies secretly run away with your details when you’re using TomTom products.
When you are using TomTom AmiGO, we want you to understand what personal data we collect from you and how we are using it. We also tell you how you can control what we do with your data. With your data you’re helping TomTom make better products that help you to reach your destination, safely and comfortably.
This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it and how you can update, manage and delete your information. This statement was last edited
on 30 November 2021 and subject to change at any time with or without further notice
. We recommend that you revisit this information from time to time to see if it has been updated.
