THE DOW JONES PRIVACY NOTICE
Effective Date: January 18, 2023
1. INTRODUCTION
Dow Jones & Company, Inc. and its subsidiaries ("Dow Jones", “our”, “us”, or “we”) want you to be familiar with how we collect, use and disclose information from and about you because we respect your concerns about privacy, and we want to maintain your trust.
This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use and disclose personal data about you when you use our websites, mobile sites, applications ("apps"), and other online products and services that link to this Privacy Notice, subscribe to our print newspaper, attend our conferences or events, and receive our  newsletters or emails (collectively, the "Dow Jones Services"). If there is a conflict between this Privacy Notice and a Dow Jones Services corporate subscription agreement, the Dow Jones Services corporate subscription agreement will take precedence.
By using the Dow Jones Services, you acknowledge you have read and accept this Privacy Notice.
The Dow Jones Services are intended for general audiences and we do not knowingly collect personal data from minors under 16 years of age.
2. DATA WE COLLECT
Dow Jones collects personal data to offer services you have requested or that we believe are of interest to you, to manage the relationships we have with our customers and our partners, and to perform activities based on your consent.  Dow Jones may obtain information about you from a variety of sources.  This includes personal data you provide to us directly, data we obtain from other sources, and data we gather through automated means.
2.1 Data You Provide to Us
We collect personal data that you provide to us when you interact with the Dow Jones Services, including the following:
Contact or identifier data when you sign up for Dow Jones Services, such as your name, postal address (including billing and shipping address), email address, telephone and/or mobile phone number;
Transactional and financial information to process transactions, such as credit or debit card number and related payment information;
Information you provide when you register for an event or conference, such as your occupation or interests;
User-generated content when you participate in a community forum or otherwise post content that others can read or view, such as your comments, username or profile picture;
Information you provide when you create a profile, participate in a sweepstakes, contest or game or take a survey, such as your education, investments, other financial information, interests, gender;
Information you provide when you use an interactive feature (such as text, chat, chat bot or article sharing options) or scan or use our QR codes, such as your mobile phone number; and
Customer service information, such as messages and other information you address to us through online forms, text, chat, by email, over the phone or by postal mail.
2.2 Data We Receive from Other Sources
We may receive personal data about you in connection with the Dow Jones Services from publicly and commercially available sources, Dow Jones Affiliates (defined in Section 4.1), business partners (such as social networking services), and, if applicable to you, the third party provider of your subscription (such as your employer or educational institution). Such personal data may include:
Purchasing data, including information about your transactions and purchase history;
Demographic data, such as gender, age range, educational level, household income range;
Occupational data, such as profession, position, title and industry;
Geolocation data; and
Your activities on other Internet sites or mobile apps, such as information about content, products, services or advertisements you have seen or interacted with, including the content, products or services of our advertisers.
If you log in with or connect a social networking profile with a Dow Jones Service (e.g., Facebook), we may obtain information about you, such as your user ID and/or user name and other data you have permitted those networks to share with us consistent with your privacy settings on the applicable social networking service. When you access the Dow Jones Services through social networking services or when you connect a Dow Jones Service to social networking services, you are authorizing Dow Jones to collect, store, disclose, and use such information and content in accordance with this Privacy Notice.
2.3 Data Collected By Automated Means Through Our Services
We gather by automated means (such as cookies, web beacons, web server logs and similar technologies) certain information through the Dow Jones Services.  Such data may include:
Your IP address;
Device information, such as unique identifier and connection information, including mobile device advertising IDs (e.g., Apple IDFA or Google AAID);
Your device type and settings, software used, browser type and operating system;
Language preferences;
Viewing behavior;
Web pages and advertisements you view and links you click on within the Dow Jones Services; and
Other usage data, such as the websites or other services you visited before and after visiting the Dow Jones Services, the content you view on our sites, how long you spent on a page, access times and other details about your use and interactions on our Services.
For more information on the various types of automated means we use to gather information, please see our
Cookie Notice
.
3. HOW WE USE THE DATA WE OBTAIN
We may use the personal data we collect from and/or about you for the business and commercial purposes set forth below.
3.1 To Provide and Manage the Dow Jones Services
This may include:
Provide our products and services, including to process and fulfill your subscription, or complete a transaction that you requested;
Create and manage online accounts;
Provide customer support, including via online chat or chat bot, and to respond to inquiries;
Send information about your relationship or transactions with us;
Provide technical support;
Deliver relevant content and suggestions (e.g., if you use multiple products, we may use your reading history or activity to personalize the content delivered to you across products);
Conduct research for potential articles if you choose to respond to a journalism survey;
Communicate about, and administer participation in, online games, contests, promotions, special events, rewards programs, surveys or market research;
If you provide your mobile phone number to us, we may ask for your consent to receive text message alerts from us containing product, service, event, or promotional information in a text or SMS message (“Text Messages”). Your consent to receive Text Messages is not required to purchase goods or services from us.
If you have multiple product subscriptions, we may use your information to allow you to sign in to several Dow Jones websites or apps with the same user ID.
3.2 To Measure And Improve Our Products And Services
We strive to improve the Dow Jones Services through various means, such as performing data and statistical analysis on user subscriptions, purchases and usage behaviors.
3.3 To Allow You To Share User-Generated Content
Certain features on the Dow Jones Services give you an opportunity to interact with us and others.  These features include collaboration tools, community forums, chats, submitting a letter to the editor, creating community profiles, rating, tagging, and commenting on articles. If you choose to use these features, any information you choose to share (such as your name, user name, email address, picture or image, likes, status, and comments) becomes public.  We are not responsible for any information you choose to submit and make public through these interactive features.

5.3.2.3  CPRA Privacy Rights
In addition to rights of access, correction and deletion, as described above, California residents may have certain rights under the CPRA, many of which are subject to limitations or exceptions:
Right to access/know and disclosure and portability
You may have the right to request, twice during a twelve month period, the following information that we may have collected about you for the time period from the date of your request to January 1, 2022:
the categories and specific pieces of personal data we may have collected about you;
the categories of sources from which we collected the personal data;
the business or commercial purpose for which we collected or sold the personal data;
the categories of third parties with whom we shared the personal data;
the categories of personal data that we sold or disclosed for a business or commercial purpose,
the categories of third parties to whom we sold or disclosed personal data for a business or commercial purpose; and
the specific pieces of personal data we obtained from you in a format that is easily understandable.
Right to nondiscrimination
We will not deny, charge different prices for, or provide a different level of quality of goods or services if you choose to exercise these rights.
Right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal data
You have the right to opt out of the “sale” or “sharing” of your personal data with third parties who are not our service providers. California law broadly defines “sale” such that it may include allowing third parties to receive certain information, such as IP address and/or browsing behavior to deliver interest-based (also called targeted) advertising, or the sharing of a print mailing address with unaffiliated third party companies whose products and services may be of interest to you.
We do not knowingly “sell” or “share” (as defined under the CPRA) the personal data of minors under 16 years old.
Right to limit the use or disclosure of sensitive personal data
You may have the right to request that we limit the use or disclosure of your sensitive personal data (e.g. account numbers, racial or ethnic information) to uses necessary to provide the Dow Jones Services to you and for purposes permitted by applicable law (e.g. to ensure security and integrity).  Dow Jones does not use sensitive personal data outside of these limited purposes.
