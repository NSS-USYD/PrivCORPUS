About RouterKeygen
RouterKeygen is a free WPA/WEP key generator for several routers around the world.
1. Scan networks and generates keys
It simply scans all networks around you and checks if it can generate possible keys for the networks.
2. Test keys
Once you have the generated keys, one or more, you can test them and luckly connect to the network.
