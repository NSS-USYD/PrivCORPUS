Privacy Policy
This privacy policy relates to our mobile applications ("apps").
No Collection of Personal Information
We do not collect any personnaly identifiable information about you when you are using our applications. 
							Under no circumstances do we collect, download or otherwise make copies of any images you make using our 
							photography applications.
Collection of Information for Statistical Purposes
Our apps may automatically collect anonymous information regarding app usage using third-party SDKs (for example Google Analytics or Google Firebase Analytics)
							to help us understand how our users use the Application. None of this information identifies you personally.
Our apps may give you the option to opt out of this statistical information gathering process, via its settings.
