Super easy drag and pose
Pose human models by simply tapping on control points and dragging. Our physics engine allows you to manipulate the 3D model like a real doll and automatically adjusts it to the dynamic poses you want.

Stunning lighting effects
Add cinematic touch to your scene by setting up multiple lights, adjusting light color, strength and a range of parameters.
