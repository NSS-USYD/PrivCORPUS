BOSS Tuner App
Chromatic Tuner App
Google Play
App Store
An authentic BOSS tuner for your smartphone or tablet
The BOSS Tuner app brings BOSS’s leading tuning technology to your favorite iOS and Android mobile devices. Available as a free download, the convenient app features the familiar look and intuitive display style of the best-selling TU-3 and TU-3W pedal tuner. Using the mic in your device, you can tune a guitar, bass, and other instruments like violin, cello, brass, etc. You can also use it to check pitch accuracy as you practice singing. In addition to chromatic tuning, the app can sound reference pitches for tuning by ear. And with the included newsfeed, you’ll always stay up to date with the latest info on BOSS products.
The BOSS Tuner app is available on the App Store and Google Play.
BOSS’s famous chromatic tuning technology in a free mobile app
Features the look and interface style of the top-selling TU-3 and TU-3W Chromatic Tuner
Provides hands-free chromatic tuning with a 21-segment meter, note display, and visual tuning indicators
Tune instruments and voices using the built-in mic in your mobile device
Audible reference pitch function for tuning by ear
Customizable skin (TU-3/TU-3W) and background color
Newsfeed provides info on BOSS products
Compatible with iOS and Android devices

Privacy Policy for smart device application BOSS Tuner
The software application BOSS tuner ("Application") uses the built-in microphone of the installed device to detect and display pitch.
Any information collected through the microphone is used only for the pitch display in the Application, and is never recorded, sent outside the device, or used for collecting personal identifiable information.
You can set the device to deny the access to the built-in microphone from the Application.In that case, the Application cannot detect and display pitch.If you wish to use the Application, allow the access to the microphone.
This Privacy Policy is subject to change in accordance with the Application update.By using the Application, you are consenting to the Privacy Policy as set forth now and as amended by us.
