User generated content
Podcast Addict users can generate content by reviewing podcasts. Those reviews are available through the app and on the website.
It is forbidden for anyone to scrap those reviews and any other Podcast Addict data like the popular podcasts lists, subscriptions and download numbers without the permission of Podcast Addict.
So by using Podcast Addict app and website you agree that you will not develop, support or use software devices, scripts, robots or any other means or processes (including crawlers, browser plugins and add-ons or any other technology) to scrape the Services or otherwise copy reviews and other data from the Services.
Privacy Policy
Thank you for using Podcast Addict!
Podcast Addict respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Podcast Addict collects, uses, and transfers your information, and how you can control the use of the information that Podcast Addict collects.
What we collect
Podcast Addict does not collect any personal information.
Feed urls, subscriptions, downloads, streaming information are collected anonymously in order to improve the app database, podcasts recommendation, and other services. If you manually add an RSS feed into the app that you don't want to share with the app database, make sure to select the '
PRIVATE
' checkbox.
Analytics
Anonymous usage statistics are collected through
Google Analytics/Firebase
in order to improve the app. You can disable them from Settings/About (
Privacy policy
).
Debug
Podcast Addict uses
Firebase Crashlytics
in order to be notified when the app crashes. Firebase Crashlytics collects some anonymous information like the stack trace, device model, android version, ... which is used by Podcast Addict team in order to fix bugs (Terms of Services).
Podcast Addict team may ask you for some information when trying to debug an issue. This information will only be used to solve that issue. None of the information gathered during the debug process will be shared with any 3rd party.
Google Drive Sign-in
This is an optional feature of the app that allows you to authenticate yourself to store a copy of the app data on your own Google Drive account.
The app doesn't store this data on its server. Your name and email address are not stored on the app server. The email address is only displayed in the app settings screen to remind you about the Google account used to sign-in.
The app only store the token Id on its server.
YouTube
By using Podcast Addict to subscribe to YouTube Channels, users acknowledge and agree that the app uses YouTube API Services. As such, users of Podcast Addict are agreeing to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service (https://www.youtube.com/t/terms), Privacy Policy (https://www.google.com/policies/privacy), and API Services Terms of Service (https://developers.google.com/youtube/terms/api-services-terms-of-service) when engaging with YouTube content on the app.
We take no responsibility for the content or privacy practices of any Third-Party Services. We encourage you to carefully review the privacy policies of any Third-Party Services you access.

Since v2020.6, the app allows the user to rate and review podcasts.
Those reviews are stored on Podcast Addict servers. Users can delete their reviews at any time.
Information usage
We use the information we collect to operate and improve our website, apps, and customer support.
We do not share personal information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to accomplish Podcast Addict functionality. We may share anonymous, aggregate statistics with outside parties, such as how many people listen to a particular podcast via Podcast Addict.
We may disclose your information in response to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal requirements; to exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud or abuse, violations of our policies; or to protect our rights and property.
In the future, we may sell to, buy, merge with, or partner with other businesses. In such transactions, user information may be among the transferred assets.
Third-party links / content
Podcasts are NOT hosted by Podcast Addict. Podcast authors are in charge of hosting their podcast RSS feed and audio/video files. So every time you update a podcast, stream or download an episode the podcast author will be able to retrieve some anonymous data on their server.
The information they can retrieve is the same as when you access the episode from your web browser, so they can trace your IP address, device and OS detail, the version of Podcast addict you're using and other information they can gather from the IP address such as your location.
We have no responsibility or liability for their content or activities.
