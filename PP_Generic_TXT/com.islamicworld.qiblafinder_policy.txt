Qibla Finder – Privacy Policy
May 8, 2018
April 15, 2019
/
NimdA
This privacy policy governs your use of the software application
Qibla Finder
for mobile devices that was created by Islamic World. The Application is an Islamic application to find Qibla direction and Islamic Prayer TImes/
The application obtains the following information from your device and given along are the reasons:
Automatically Collected Information
Location
– The permission for Location is required in order to find Qibla direction and islamic prayer times for the location.
Device ID & call information
Read phone status and identity – This permission is required to ensure the application starts from the same state if you receive a call while using app.
