When user visits the Website again, the cookie allows that site to recognize user’s browser. Cookies may store user preferences and other information. User can reset his browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some Website features or services may not function properly without cookies.

(d) The Website or third party service providers, advertisers, advertising networks and platforms, agencies, also may use cookies or other tracking technologies to manage and measure the performance of advertising displayed on or delivered by or through other network or services.
