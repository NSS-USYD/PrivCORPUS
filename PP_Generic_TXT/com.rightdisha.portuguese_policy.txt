This privacy policy sets out how Right Disha uses and protects any information that you give Right Disha when you use this app.
Right
 Disha is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Should 
we ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified
 when using this website, then you can be assured that it will only be 
used in accordance with this privacy statement.
Right Disha may 
change this policy from time to time by updating this page. You should 
check this page from time to time to ensure that you are happy with any 
changes.
What we collect
In order to improve the performance, User experience and monetary purpose we have integrated third party implementations.
We may collect the following information:
Advertising Id, Android id, Google account profile,
Geographic and Demographic data,
Search history on those platforms
We added some third parties integration such as Google Admob, Facebook SDK. So, these services may get following information.
App permissions required,
INTERNET
Internet is required to serve the advertisments.
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
To check the current state of network.
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
Allow to write external storage data
What we do with the information we gather
We do not store any information at our end. We use this anonymous 
information to serve you better. This data is being used with our third 
party networks to serve the appropriate ads and push notifications.
We do not have your personal information.
For more details what our partner do with data could be found here.
Google account privacy policy could be found at,
https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en&gl=ZZ
For more information about what Facebook data collect go through this link,
https://www.facebook.com/about/privacy/update/printable
Security
We
 are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to 
prevent unauthorized access or disclosure we have put in place suitable 
physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure 
the information we collect online.
