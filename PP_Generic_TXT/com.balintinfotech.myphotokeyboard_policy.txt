To make better experience with native language use, apps need to permission of read user dictionary so that user can define and use, reuse of words in keyboard.

WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE to store user capture or selected photo for keyboard background.
android.permission.INTERNET, android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE,
android.permission.ACCESS_WIFI_STATE to get online themes of keyboard by web services.
