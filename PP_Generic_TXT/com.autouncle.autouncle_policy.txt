Introduction
This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can update, manage, export, and delete your information.
We build a range of products in our vision to help car buyers and sellers make hassle free deals happen. Our services include:
AutoUncle websites and apps
Products for car dealers like AutoUncle Analytics and AutoUncle Leadbox
Products that are integrated into third-party sites like AutoUncle rating widgets and AutoUncle Trade-in module

What Information Does AutoUncle Collect?
We collect information to provide better services to all our users – from buyers looking for the perfect deal to sellers looking for an effective sale. Here are three types of information we gather:
Information you give us:
we receive and store information you provide in relation to AutoUncle Services. Examples of information is your email, user account, cars you are interested in, cars you own, messages to buyers and sellers that you send and so on. You can choose not to provide certain information but then you might not be able to take advantage of many of our AutoUncle Services. In connection with the submission of your data it will always be stated whether the submission is voluntary or necessary for completing the desired action.
Automatic Information:
we automatically receive and store certain types of information when you use AutoUncle Services, such as information about your use, including your interaction with content and services available through AutoUncle Services. Like many websites, we use cookies and other unique identifiers to deliver and improve services. We collect information such as IP-address, referrer URL of your request and unique identifiers like your browser’s User-Agent, your device’s location and crash reports.
Information that is publicly available online:
we index and store relevant information that is publicly available on the Internet. Examples of information are data about cars for sale on other websites and the seller’s contact data (if available). We carefully anonymise or delete personal information of this type when the original form is no longer required.
Social Media services:
AutoUncle allows you to sign up and log in using your Facebook account. If you sign up via Facebook, Facebook will ask your permission to share your authentication information from your Facebook account with AutoUncle. This includes your first name, last name, e-mail address, and encrypted access credentials. We may store this information for the purposes explained in this statement, and it may also be used to verify your credentials with Facebook for some of the features on AutoUncle (such as sharing a car’s page on Facebook). This information is collected by Facebook and is provided to us under the terms of
Facebook’s privacy policy
. You can control the information we receive from Facebook using the privacy settings in your Facebook account.
For What Purposes Does AutoUncle Process Your Personal Information?
We process your personal information to operate, provide, and improve the AutoUncle Services that we offer our customers. These purposes include:
Searching for cars. We use your personal information to help you find cars that match your interest.
Selling cars. We use your personal information to help you advertise cars.
Calculate market value of cars. We use your personal information to help you calculate the market price and potential selling time of your own or other cars.
Communicate with buyer or seller. We use your personal information to help facilitate communication between buyer and seller.
Provide, troubleshoot, and improve AutoUncle Services. We use your personal information to provide functionality, analyse performance, fix errors, and improve usability and effectiveness of the AutoUncle Services.
Recommendations and personalisation. We use your personal information to recommend features, products, and services that might be of interest to you, identify your preferences, and personalise your experience with AutoUncle Services.
Communicate with you. We use your personal information to communicate with you in relation to AutoUncle Services via different channels (e.g., by phone, email, chat).
Advertising. We use your personal information to display interest-based ads for features, products, and services that might be of interest to you.
Purposes for which we seek your consent. We may also ask for your consent to process your personal information for a specific purpose that we communicate to you. When you consent to our processing your personal information for a specified purpose, you may withdraw your consent at any time and we will stop processing of your data for that purpose.

Does AutoUncle Share Your Personal Information?
We do not share your personal information with companies, organisations, or individuals outside of AutoUncle except in the following cases:
With your consent:
We’ll share personal information outside of AutoUncle when we have your consent. For example if you wish to submit a trade-in request to a car dealer on AutoUncle or the car dealer's website or request a quote for financing a car.
With Third Party Service Providers:
We employ other companies and individuals to perform functions on our behalf. Examples include analysing data, processing payments, securely dispatching emails and developing or optimising AutoUncle Services.
Required by law:
Under specific circumstances and with reference to legislation, it might be necessary to transfer information to public authorities or the police.
Your personal data is disclosed to partners of AutoUncle who deliver services on our behalf, for example secure ways to host and backup data, services for secure and reliable email delivery. These partners only process the personal data on behalf of AutoUncle and in accordance with the instructions of AutoUncle.
How Secure is Information About Me?
We design our systems with your security and privacy in mind.
We work to protect the security of your information during transmission by using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software, which encrypts information you input.
We review our information collection, storage, and processing practices, to prevent unauthorized access to our systems
We restrict access to personal information to AutoUncle employees, contractors, and agents who need that information in order to process it. Anyone with this access is subject to strict contractual confidentiality obligations and may be disciplined or terminated if they fail to meet these obligations.
It is important for you to protect against unauthorised access to your password and to your computers, devices, and applications. Be sure to sign off when you finish using a shared computer.
What Choices Do I Have?
As described above, you can choose not to provide certain information but then you might not be able to take advantage of many of the AutoUncle Services.
If you don’t wish to receive e-emails from us, please unsubscribe from a link at the bottom of an email or by going to your user settings.
If you don’t wish to receive in-app notifications from us, please adjust your notification settings in the app or your device.
In addition, subject to applicable law, you have the right to request access to, correct, and delete your personal data, and to ask for data portability. You may also object to our processing of your personal data or ask that we restrict the processing of your personal data in certain instances. You are at any time entitled to be informed of the personal data about you that we process, but with certain legislative exceptions.
You may, at any time, withdraw any consent you have provided and we will delete your personal data, unless we can continue the processing based on another purpose.
If you wish to do any of these things, please contact
privacy@autouncle.com
.
How Long Do We Keep Your Personal Information?
We keep your personal information to enable your continued use of AutoUncle Services, for as long as it is required in order to fulfil the relevant purposes described in this Privacy Policy. We automatically expire some types of Personal Information when it is no longer essential for the delivery of AutoUncle services. For example, we carefully anonymize visitor tracking from any IP or User-Agent after some time.
Does AutoUncle Store Personal Information Outside of EU?
AutoUncle generally never store personal information outside of the European Union (EU). Our services and the personal information that we store are hosted and delivered from servers in EU.
In the event that we are forced to transfer data outside of EU, we will ensure that the information is transferred in accordance with this Privacy Notice and as permitted by the applicable laws on data protection.
