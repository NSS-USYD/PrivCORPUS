GpsFix
GpsFix helps to decrease the time required to obtain a position fix. It keeps GPS receiver turned on until location is fixed.
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.androidlab.gpsfix
Privacy Policy
GpsFix does not collect or transfer any data to us or to any third party apart from the circumstances explained below.
Location
GpsFix uses your phone's location only to show your current location.
