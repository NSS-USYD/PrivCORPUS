Privacy Policy
The GB What’s app was created as a Free app by us. This SERVICE is given free of charge by us and is intended to be used as is.
If someone decides to use our Service, this page is meant to inform visitors about our policies regarding the collection, use, and sharing of Personal Information.
Unless otherwise defined in this Privacy Policy, the terminology used in this Privacy Policy have the same meanings as in our Terms & Conditions, which may be found at GB What’s.

Information Collection and Use
We do not gather any personally identifiable information. Third-party services may collect information that can be used to identify you.
Link to the privacy policies of the app's third-party service providers.
    • Google Play Services
    • AdMob
    • Google Analytics for Firebase
    • Firebase Crashlytics

Log Data
We want to let you know that whenever you use our Service, we collect data and information (or through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data in the event of an app issue. This Log Data may comprise information such as your device name, operating system version, app configuration when using our Service, time and date of service use, and other data.

Cookies
Cookies are little data files that are frequently utilised as anonymous unique identifiers. These are transmitted to your browser from the websites you visit and saved on the internal memory of your device.
These "cookies" are not specifically used by this Service. Third-party code and libraries that utilise "cookies" to collect information and improve their services may be used by the app. You can choose to accept or reject these cookies, and you'll be notified when one is sent to your device. You may not be able to utilise some parts of this Service if you refuse our cookies.



Links to Other Sites
There may be links to other websites on this Service. You will be taken to a third-party site if you click on a third-party link. Please keep in mind that we do not control these third-party websites. As a result, we strongly suggest that you read the Privacy Policies of these websites. We have no control over, and take no responsibility for, any third-party sites or services, including their content, privacy policies, or practises.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
From time to time, we may make changes to our Privacy Policy. As a result, you should check this page for updates on a regular basis. Any changes will be communicated to you by posting the revised Privacy Policy on this page. These modifications take effect as soon as they are published on this page.
