LGANA Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
We care about your privacy.
This Privacy Policy applies to your usage of
the software and services provided by LGANA.
We don't collect any personal information, we
only collect some information about your device and will never sell or share
this information with other legal entities for commercial purposes,
except in the cases when it's required as a
part of the Services you requested.
The information we collect and how we use it
The information you provide
Every time you login to the Chat rooms, servers log an entry that includes date
and time, your IP address, your guest or registered nickname, client platform,
client software version, device model and serial numbers. We use this
information for analytical purposes in aggregated form.
Public channels
When you login to a public chat room, your nickname is visible to other users
connected to the same room and only Administrators can see your IP Address and
know your country based on it.
Communication privacy
We do not record or keep track of your voice , video or text communication in
using the Service.
Disclosure
Services may disclose personally identifiable information when it's required to
comply with law enforcement requests or subpoenas when you violate the Terms of
Service.
