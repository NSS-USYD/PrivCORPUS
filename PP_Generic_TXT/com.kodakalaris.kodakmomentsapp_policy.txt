Information Collected And Used When You Install Partner Apps
No information is collected by Kodak Alaris when you install a Partner app. Please refer to the individual Partner app’s privacy policy to determine what information the partner collects.
Information Collected And Used When You Create or Order a Photo Product On-line
When you are in the process of working on a photo product in the prints workflow, the Alaris App is using a Kodak Alaris server. A Kodak Alaris session id is stored locally on your mobile device so that you can continue working on photo products using the application. This information is deleted if you start the application again. Session information is deleted after the order has been placed.
When you are in the process of working on a photo product, the following information regarding each selected photo is also sent to Kodak Alaris: the photo image and its associated image tags that are contained in the image file. This information is temporarily stored at Kodak Alaris and then deleted after 2 days with no continued activity, or when you begin using the Alaris App again. When you print a photo product, this information is temporarily stored at Kodak Alaris to support your current order and to permit you to make a repeat future order. It is deleted after 30 days. When you store and share a photo product, this information is temporarily stored at Kodak Alaris and then deleted when you delete the photo product that you stored and shared.
When you submit for printing a photo product, the Alaris App may ask you for identification information. This data may include your first name, last name, telephone number, and e-mail address. This data will be associated with your photo product order and then deleted 6 months after the order is completed or cancelled.
When using Partner applications, Kodak Alaris collects the images, order information and data that may include your first name, last name, telephone number and e-mail address. This data will be associated with your photo product order and then deleted 6 months after the order is completed or cancelled.

Information Alaris Apps Automatically Collect And How This Information Is Used
At installation time, you may be asked for your consent to track your usage of the Alaris App. This data will be recorded in the form of Cookies and web beacons. We use Localytics, a web analysis service, to track your usage of the Alaris Apps. If you do not consent to using performance cookies for usage tracking, the analytics function will not collect information.
Cookies: Cookies are small pieces of information stored by the Alaris App in your mobile device for the purposes set out below.
Kodak Alaris “strictly necessary cookies” are used primarily as follows:
to manage and present site information and the pictures displayed on your computer;
to deliver information specific to your interests; and
to manage images associated with photo products like on a shopping cart screen.
Our Alaris Apps use Localytics, a web analysis service. If you have given your consent, Localytics uses “performance cookies,” which are text files placed on your mobile device, to help the Alaris App analyze how users use the Alaris App. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the Alaris App will be transmitted to and stored by Localytics on servers in the United States, a country which does not provide for the same level of data protection as considered adequate in the European Union. However, we have appropriate safeguards in place to ensure that Localytics will use this information only on behalf of Kodak Alaris, as the Alaris App operator, and only for the purpose of evaluating your use of the Alaris App, compiling reports on Alaris App activity, and providing other services to Kodak Alaris App relating to Alaris App activity and Internet usage.
Some Alaris Apps also use Fabric, a web analysis service from Twitter inc. that transmits information about your use of the app. Fabric collects information and statistics and crash data about from your use of the Alaris app. This information does not contain your personal information, and is used by us to improve the app.
You may prevent the use of Localytics and Fabric by unselecting the appropriate setting in the Alaris App’s setup screen.
Web Beacons: Alaris Apps also may place small "tracker gifs" or "beacons" on many of the screens in our Alaris App. With your consent, we use these beacons, in connection with cookies, to collect data on the usage of our Alaris Apps including but not limited to the date and time of your usage, the screens or functions used, the type of operating system (e.g., iOS or Android), and the domain name of the user's Internet service provider (e.g., Verizon). This information is collected about thousands of Alaris App uses and analyzed as a whole. This information is useful in, for example, tracking the performance of our Alaris App.
When using WhatsApp ordering photo products
Description and scope of data processing
With the Kodak Alaris Chat & Print service on WhatsApp, Kodak Alaris offers the end customers of its partners the ability to order photo products from its partners via WhatsApp. Provided that the end customers have given the corresponding declaration of consent, they can contact Kodak Alaris via their WhatsApp channel and send images to Kodak Alaris partners for processing. When images are transmitted via WhatsApp to Kodak Alaris' partners, personal data in the form of the images may be processed by WhatsApp. Kodak Alaris and its partners themselves do not transmit any personal data to WhatsApp (neither do they transmit telephone numbers of end customers which enable them to contact Kodak Alaris in the first place, but which are already available at WhatsApp, nor do they send images from Kodak Alaris to WhatsApp or other data such as address data).
By agreeing to be contacted by Kodak Alaris and its partners via WhatsApp, the end customer agrees to the transmission path to the described risks. Kodak Alaris, as owner of the service on WhatsApp, cannot delete the telephone number transmitted by the end customer. The transmitted images can be deleted by Kodak Alaris upon request of the end customer. Otherwise the transmitted images are automatically deleted after 7 days. The terms of use of WhatsApp can be found at https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/?eea=1#terms-of-service. The privacy policy of WhatsApp Inc. can be found here: https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/?eea=1#privacy-policy.
Other personal data is not transferred via WhatsApp during the order process. Please do not enter any personal information into WhatsApp without being asked.
Legal basis, objection and deletion
The communication between the end customer and the partners of Kodak Alaris via WhatsApp is based on the explicit consent of the end customer to this transmission process, legal basis is Art. 6 para. 1 a) DSGVO/GDPR. Kodak Alaris has no influence on the transmission to WhatsApp, the storage, deletion and general processing of this data on and by WhatsApp.
With regard to the order the partners of Kodak Alaris process the data of the end customer on the basis of a contract; legal basis for the processing of the data is Art. 6 para. 1 b) DSGVO/GDPR. Within the scope of order processing the partners of Kodak Alaris pass on the personal data of the end customers to the service providers used for the processing of the photo orders. The data about this order (like name, address, telephone number, payment data and picture data) are stored for the purpose of the order processing as well as the treatment of possible complaints and repeat orders with the partners and are deleted afterwards. The storage period at the partners of Kodak Alaris for personal data will be up to 10 years, due to tax and commercial law obligations (if an order was placed). The period of storage starts on 1st of January of the year following the order and ends 10 years later. The data will be deleted accordingly after this period
