What is a privacy policy?
Privacy Policy of an application is a legal policy that states how an app is going to handle and process the data of its user. It is an explicit commitment made by the app owner that describes whether the data will be confidential or will be sold to a third party. Overall, Privacy-Policy makes your experience smooth and easy if one reads and understands the rules thoroughly!
