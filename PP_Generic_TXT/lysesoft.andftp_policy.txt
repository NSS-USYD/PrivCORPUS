Web Site
The web server collects and saves only the
                            default information customarily logged by server
                            software. For each http request received, the date
                            and time, the originating IP address, the object
                            requested, and the completion status of the request
                            is collected and saved.
We analyze these logs to determine the amount of
                            traffic through the server, the number of pages
                            served, the level of demand for various pages and
                            referral information. We summarize the logs to
                            determine statistical information on the type of
                            browsers and operating systems that are being used
                            to access our pages.
