App Privacy policy
Free app [Ad-supported version]
We care about your privacy and data security. We keep this app free by showing ads.
[This paragraph only applies in EURO-GDPR countries] You can choose to use the app for free by agreeing to 1) see personalised ads - relevant, based on your past behaviour - or 2) non-personalized ads - not based on your past behavior.
[This paragraph only applies in non-EURO-GDPR countries] You can choose to use the app free by agreeing to see ads.
You can also choose to buy a paid version of the app which is ad-free. This version is available in Google Play.
Your device has a unique advertising identifier. We might share it with our advertising partners to serve ads to our apps.

App will try to get your country based on your network connection to configure certain features of the app. An external webservice might be used. Your location will NOT be tracked
[This paragraph only applies in Quran Apps] Auto-location feature is optional and will help you setting your current city and country to configure prayer times. It will try to get your approximate location based on your WiFi/data connection. An external webservice might be used. This is an optional action to configure the app - your location will NOT be tracked
You can check your choice or review this terms anytime in the app by clicking on Privacy icon (Main Screen -> Top-right menu -> Privacy).
