Refund Policy
Our mission is to provide quality and relevant learning to students across the globe. However, we
                    understand that not all content is exactly relevant for everyone. Therefore, students purchasing
                    courses
                    on Vidyakul are protected under a 7 day refund policy.
This policy is to ensure that courses on Vidyakul provide full satisfaction and value to students.
                    Students not satisfied with the course for any reason can get full refund on their purchase
                    (excluding
                    payment gateway charges).
How to request for refund:
Students who need a refund should send an e-mail to “info@vidyakul.com” with the following
Name of the course
Your Vidyakul account email
Course purchase confirmation receipt and
The reason(s) for refund.
Your request for refund will be processed for approval with 2 weeks of your email. Once approved, it
                    might take up to 4 weeks for the money to be credited to your account. Students can also choose to
                    get
                    the money refunded as Vidyakul credit that could be used for future course purchases. Students
                    choosing
                    for Vidyakul credit would get a refund within 3 days after approval.Exceptions:
While we strive to provide refunds to all students who do not find it satisfactory, however there are
                    few
                    cases where refunds could be revoked
If a student participates in the entire course or has downloaded the course before the refund
                        was
                        requested, the refund might be not approved
Students who wish to purchase a course after claiming refund on that course before are not
                        eligible
                        for refund on the same course again
Students who purchase and refund multiple courses over a period of time
Our refund policy is offered to protect students get the right value from our platform and evaluate
                    the
                    course. However, we also need to protect our instructors from fraud and provide a reasonable
                    expectation
                    of being paid. Therefore, we have limited the time for refund. We do NOT offer refunds for courses
                    after
                    the 7 day refund period.
ACCOUNT DELETION
If you wish to delete your account, please send an email to our customer care team at info@vidyakul.com. Our team will review your request and reach out to you for confirmation. Upon your confirmation, we will delete your account within 96 hours.
