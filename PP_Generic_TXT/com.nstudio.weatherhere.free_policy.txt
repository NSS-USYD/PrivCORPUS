Granite Apps Privacy policy
Granite Apps LLC does not collect personally identifiable information about its
            users except to the extent required by the features of the app and in providing
            support for these features. In all other cases data collected by the app is stored
            on the device only.
Some features, such as providing severe weather alerts, require the users device to
            send location information to Granite Apps servers so that it can send the appropriate
            weather alerts. The information is not used for other purposes and the data is not
            collected if the user does not enable these features.
