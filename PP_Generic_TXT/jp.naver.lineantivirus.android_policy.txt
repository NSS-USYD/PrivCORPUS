LINE Antivirus Privacy Policy
Collection and Use of Information
LINE Antivirus collects information on installed applications such as application names, package names, hashes, signatures, versions.
It also collects information on your device, such as carrier, country/region, model name, and Android OS details. This information is only used to analyze, diagnose, and remove malicious code and is never. All information collected will be encrypted and transmitted to the LINE antivirus log collection server. All collected information is only used improve the LINE Antivirus service and its features.
URLs of websites you visit are collected to check for and provide notifications about dangerous websites.
