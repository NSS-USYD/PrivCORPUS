Privacy Policy
MediBang Inc.  (here referred to as the "Company") has established the following privacy policy (here referred to as the "Policy") regarding the handling of personal information on each website operated by the Company and each application distributed by the Company (here referred to as the "Service").
1.Personal Information
For the purpose of the Policy, personal information is defined as information about an individual that the Company collects in accordance with the Policy when the User uses the Service, and information that can identify the specific individual contained in the information (excluding personal identification codes). The term "personal information" refers to information about individuals that the Company collects based on the Policy when Users use the Service, and that can be used to identify specific individuals (excluding personal identification codes, but including information that can be easily cross-checked with other information to identify specific individuals). The term "personal information" refers to information about a user that is collected by the Company.
Information about users that the company may collect through the service includes the following:
Information provided at the time of account creation
・E-mail address, date of birth, user name, and password
・Information provided by users who agree to link with other services (Google, Facebook, Twitter, etc.) (user IDs, registered email addresses, and other information that the user has authorized to be disclosed according to the privacy settings of the service in question)
Information generated by use of the Service (including information about advertisements clicked on prior to visiting each website)
・Equipment information (operating system, PC name, device information), IP address, browser information, language selected, country, region
・Activity history such as URL visited, content, reference order
・Cookie
・Advertising identifiers (including IDFA, AdvertisingID, etc.), time and date of clicking on advertisements on other companies' websites, information about the media used for the visit (media member ID, media member name, media website name, etc.)
Information necessary for the sale of content, shipment of products, contests, and other services provided
・Bank account information, paypal account information, SoftBank Payment
・Name, address, and telephone number
・Credit card information, and other information necessary for settlement by the payment method designated by the company
*However, the company outsources settlement operations to outside companies and agents, and will not retain the information necessary for settlement.
For this reason, please contact the respective credit card company and settlement agent for deletion of credit card information.
・SB Payment Service
・And other information provided directly through other documents, forms, etc.
Information related to inquiries
・E-mail address and user name
・Details of the inquiry
Information for recruitment activities
・Information to be included in resumes and curriculum vitae (name, age, gender, date of birth, telephone number, e-mail address, etc.)
・The contents of communications between the Company and job seekers

2.Intended use of personal information
The Company collect and process personal information only for the purposes described below:
To provide the Service.
To complete procedures such as account registration, account withdrawal, and identity verification.
To provide various communications and information regarding the Service.
To fulfill contracts such as sales of contents and delivery of products through the Service.
To operate contests and other services provided through the Service, and to pay prize money and send prizes, etc.
To conduct questionnaires, campaigns, etc.
To respond to inquiries about the Service.
To respond to violations of the Terms of Use of the Service.
To send marketing emails containing information about the Service.
To improve and upgrade the Service, and to use as decision-making materials for advertising, etc.
To create statistical data that does not identify individual users, such as users' usage patterns.
To respond to recruitment.
For the purposes of work-related communications, payment of commission expenses, preparation of payment records, investigation of prohibited acts, etc. in contracts and for purposes incidental to these purposes.
For the purposes of managing and communicating with shareholders and making notifications and reports, etc., to a government office in accordance with the provisions of laws and regulations, including the Companies Act, and for purposes incidental thereto.
For the purpose of the delivery of behavioral targeting ads through the use of ad servers such as Google, Yahoo! and others.
For the purpose of analysis of the user’s demographic information, behavioral history and other information obtained by the Company in order to identify the user’s interests and preferences.
For purposes incidental to the intended use above.
The Company may obtain information from third party tools about advertisements clicked on prior to visiting the Company’s website (time and date of click, website serving the advertisement, etc.) and may associate this data with order information to measure the effectiveness of the advertisement.
3.Limitations of use
The Company shall not use personal information obtained by users through the use of the Service beyond the scope necessary to achieve the above purposes of use, except in the following cases:
When required by law.
When it is necessary to protect the life, body, or property of an individual and it is difficult to obtain the consent of the individual.
When it is particularly necessary to improve public health or promote the sound growth of children, and it is difficult to obtain the consent of the individual.
When it is necessary to cooperate with a national agency, a local government, or an entity entrusted by either of the foregoing in executing affairs prescribed by laws and regulations, and obtaining the consent of the individual is likely to impede the execution of such affairs.
4.Implementation of appropriate security management measures
The Company shall implement necessary and appropriate security management measures with respect to personal information held by the Company to prevent unauthorized access, falsification, data leakage, loss or damage, etc. These measures include establishing a person in charge of personal information management, organizing internal discipline, training employees, monitoring access to the company, restricting equipment brought in, restricting access to information, preventing unauthorized access, etc. Please contact the address given in Section 13 for any inquiries regarding safety management measures.
5.Retention period of personal information
Personal information processed by the Company will be stored by the Company for the period necessary to achieve the purposes set forth in the Policy and to fulfill the obligations under laws and regulations.
6.Outsourcing the management of personal information
In the event that the Company consigns the handling of acquired personal information to a third party, the Company will enter into a necessary agreement with the consignor regarding the handling of personal information and supervise the consignor to ensure that the consignor manages the personal information appropriately.
7.Provision of personal information to third parties
The Company will not provide personal information to third parties without the consent of the user, except when permitted by law. However, this does not apply to the following cases:
When the Company entrusts all or part of the handling of personal information within the scope necessary to achieve the purpose of use.
When accumulating or analyzing individual personal information for the purpose of providing statistical information, processing it into a format that does not identify the individual, and disclosing the statistical data.
When providing a user's personal information to an external service for the purpose of using it in conjunction with the external service or for authentication using the external service.
When personal information is provided in connection with the succession of a business due to a merger or other reasons.
When disclosure or provision of personal information is permitted by the Personal Information Protection Law or other laws and regulations.
When there is a legal obligation to create a record when providing personal information to a third party.
8.Provision to third parties abroad
When providing personal information to third parties in foreign countries, the Company will take necessary measures in accordance with the provisions of laws and regulations, such as providing information.
9.Anonymized or pseudonymized personal information
The Company shall handle anonymized or pseudonymized personal information (referring to “anonymized personal information” and “pseudonymized personal information” as defined in the Act on the Protection of Personal Information) in accordance with the provisions of laws and regulations.
10.Requests for disclosure and correction of personal information, etc., and users’ rights regarding personal information
When users make requests based on the Act on the Protection of Personal Information regarding the personal data of such users that the Company holds, requests for notification of the purpose of use, disclosure (including disclosure of records of provision/receipt of personal data to/from third parties), correction, addition, deletion, suspension of use/erasure or cessation of provision to third parties (hereinafter referred to them as “disclosure, etc.”), the Company shall respond to the users in accordance with the Act after confirming that the request is made by the users themselves. To make such a request, please contact the address given in Section 13.
Users may withdraw their consent to the Policy at any time. Withdrawal will not, however, affect the legality of any use based on the agreement prior to withdrawal.
11.Exemption from liability
The Company shall not be held responsible for the acquisition of personal information by a third party in the following cases:
When a user reveals his/her personal information to a third party using the functions of the Service or other means.
When a user's personal information can be identified by the information the user has entered on the Service.
12.Revision of the Privacy Policy
The Company may revise all or part of the Policy without prior notice. In the event of a revision, we will notify you via the Service.
