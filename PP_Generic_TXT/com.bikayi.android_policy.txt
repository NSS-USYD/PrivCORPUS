Frequently
Asked Questions
Why do I need a Whatsapp-integrated store?
In the age of social media, if you are not promoting your products on Facebook and Whatsapp you will be missing out on a huge customer base. Bikayi helps you to set up a Whatsapp-integrated shop, so you can sell products on WhatsApp.
How do I take payments?
Bikayi offers integrations that allow you to take payments via different methods like UPI, Debit Card, etc at transaction charges starting from just 0.99 % per transaction.
What is a MINI app?
Along with an e-commerce website, Bikayi provides you with an app, which can be shared with friends, family, and customers directly from within the Bikayi app.
What is Google shop integration?
Bikayi helps you list your products on Google shop, along with integrating your Bikayi store with them. So whenever you upload or update any product on your online Bikayi store, they will be automatically updated on Google shop as well.
What is pin code-based shipping?
It is one of the most efficient features provided by the Bikayi app. With this feature, you can set different shipping charges for different Pin-codes. Hence, charging the exact rates from your customers.
What is the difference between VIP and Ultimate packages?
The VIP Package provides a number of useful features like custom themes, custom domain integration, excel upload, and many more. The Ultimate feature includes all the VIP features along with some additional features like a dedicated business coach, Google and Facebook shop integration, 0% UPI charges, and much more.
Is Bikayi free?
Yes, taking orders, inquiries, and receiving payments will always remain free with Bikayi. You just have to pay a minimal amount to increase the product limit in your e-commerce store and get your hands on numerous effective features to grow in the online market.
Can I use my own domain name?
Yes, you can connect your own personal domain easily with Bikayi. You can also get a free domain connection with Bikayi’s Yearly Package.
How to start an online business using Bikayi?
Using Bikayi is the easiest way to start your online business in a few seconds. All you have to do is create an online store by entering your mobile number and the OTP. You can then add catalogs and products to start accepting orders.
Will Bikayi provide delivery?
Bikayi has a number of integrated delivery partners, like Pickrr, WareIQ, Bluedart, Shiprocket, and many more, which provide the best delivery services in the country. You can choose and activate any of these delivery services for your online store, from within the Bikayi app.
How to remove powered by bikayi in the website?
You can integrate your custom domain to remove Biakyi branding from your e-commerce website.
Is this app helpful for a person who doesn't have the GST?
Yes, it is helpful for everyone with a dream and will to succeed in the online business industry. You don’t necessarily need to update your GST number to start your online business with Bikayi.
