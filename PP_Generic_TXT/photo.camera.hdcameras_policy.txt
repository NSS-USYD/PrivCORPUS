About our permissions
Camera for Android asks for permissions to use GET_ACCOUNTS and READ_CONTACTS feature provided by mobile device is used to is to share picture with friends, picture are safe by bringing social account to send to friends.
Camera for Android asks for CAMERA permissions is for using the camera to take photoes and record videos.
Camera for Android use the RECORD_AUDIO permissions is to record audio when recording videos.
