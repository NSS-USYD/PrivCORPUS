Double dragon privacy policy
Privacy Policy Effective
October 6, 2018
Introduction
DoubleDragon(“Developer”, “we” or “our”) created and implemented this Privacy Policy in its
organization. Its purpose is to explain to Users how Developer collects, stores, and uses your
personal information in connection with our Products and Services.
Developer’s Privacy Policy, as well as our internal policies, are fully compliant to the most recent
European Union rules deriving from the General Data Protection Regulation (EU GPDR
2016/679).
For questions and information about this Privacy Policy and Users’ rights and data protection,
please contact us at AndroidDoubleDragon@gmail.com
Developer has always the right to amend this Privacy Policy, for example in response to
mandatory requirements in the applicable law governing data protection.
Your continued use of our Products and Services means that you agree to be bound by the most
recent version of our Privacy Policy when these are changed.
If you do not agree with this Privacy and Cookie Policy, we advise you to leave the App Store and
do not use our Products and Services
1. The Information Developer Collects
We only collect personal and non-personal information once you provided us your expressed
consent and we always notify Users about the exact purpose of collecting such data. Before you
leave your personal data with us, you will have the option to provide your consent.
When downloading our
app, making in-app purchases, filing support tickets or emailing with us,
we collect and process the following data.
A. Data you provide us
● Player nickname;
● First and last name, email address;
● Other data you choose may choose to provide us.
B. Data we collect automatically
● Your IP address;
● Certain mobile device identifiers (such as your device ID, advertising ID, MAC address and
IMEI);
● Data about your device, such as device name and operating system, browser type and
language;
● Data we collect with cookies and similar technologies (see more below);
● Coarse location data (country or city-specific).
C. Purpose of data collection
The purpose of collecting this information is to complete a User’s subscription successfully on
our website and to receive payments for subscriptions and deliver the purchased merchandise.
2. Data we collect from our partners
We make use
of Third Party Service Providers that may collect, store, and use the following
additional personal and non-personal information:
● Data we receive if you link a third-party tool with our Products and Services, for example login
using your Facebook, Google or other existing user account;
● Demographic data, such as to determine the coarse location based on your IP address;
● Data to combat fraud, such as refund abuse in games;
● Data from platforms that the games run on in order to verify payment;
● Data for advertising and analytics purposes, so we can keep improving our Products and
Services;
3. The Way Developer Uses Information
We may use the collected information in order to improve our Services. If you have logged in, we
use the collected information in order to provide part of our Products and Services, for example
a leader board.
4. When Developer Discloses Information
We never disclose your personal data to any third party without your explicit consent. After you
have logged in, in order to identify yourself, we may provide limited information to other logged
in users, such as (high) scores, and player’s nickname.
5. Note About Children’s Privacy
You represent and warrant that you are 13 years or older, and that you have the legal capacity to
use our Products and Services, through the permission of your legal guardian. If we learn that we
have inadvertently collected personal data from children under the age of 13, reasonable
measures and efforts will be taken in order to purge such personal information from Developer
records without delay.
If
you are under 13, please do not send any personal or other data to us, this includes your
name, address, telephone number and email address. Nobody under the age of 13 may provide
any personal data. If we learn that we have collected personal data about a child under age 13,
we will delete that data as quickly as possible. If you believe that we might have any data from or
about a child under the age of 13, please contact us at: AndroidDoubleDragon@gmail.com
6. Third Party Privacy Policies
Developer uses the third party services to be able to provide our Services. Your access to and use
of the Services may be subject to certain third party terms and conditions and Developer is not
liable for any such third parties’ use of your personal information. The third parties whose
services are used by Developer:
● Google Admob, Analytics & Firebase (https://policies.google.com/privacy).
7. Withdrawing Consent
We only collect personal information once you have given us your expressed consent and we
shall always notify you about the exact purpose of collecting such data. Before you leave your
data with us, you will have the option to provide your consent. At all times you may withdraw
your consent and request the removal of all your personal data.
8. Security
We are committed to take all the security measures that are necessary, and which are based on
industry-wide best practices. The purpose is to protect your personal information. However, the
transmission of data and information via the internet is never completely secure. For that reason
we cannot guarantee the security of data sent to us. This means it’s entirely at your risk.
9. Data Breach
In
the event of a data breach, we act quickly to avoid and mitigate data losses and shall directly
notify you as a User, if your personal data could be at risk.
However, we do not accept any liability for privacy policies of third parties, including but not
limited to social media websites that appear on our website. We do not have any control over
the way they collect personal data or their data protection policies.
10. Internal Privacy Policies for Employees and Affiliates
We are committed to implement the highest standards within our organization regarding data
protection. This contains clear roles, duties, and responsibilities within our organization.
Employees who handle personal data in our organization our aware of their duties, and we shall
implement a privacy strategy within our organization.
