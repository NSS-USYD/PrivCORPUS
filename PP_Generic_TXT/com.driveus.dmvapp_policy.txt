DMV privacy policy
User privacy policy is very important to us.This web document 
represent the privacy policy for DMV driving motor vehicle app. We tried to cover 
the all privacy policy, security and user profile related information. 
Still if you have problem you can contact us this email.
Android permissions required by device:-
## ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
It is required to check the current state of Network of device.
## ACCESS_WIFI_STATE
The permission is used to check the wi fi state of device. If the 
internet is not on, then assist you to turn on the internet as required to 
check the DMV tests.
## INTERNET
It is required to give the exact data of nearby solution such as tyre shop. In order to see the traffic signs and images internet is required.
## WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE & READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE:-
It is essential for giving user the personalized experience.
Which data could have by third party networks?:-
1. Device related information such as manufacturer, model, Android OS version
2. Demographic data such as locations, country, city etc
3. Interest based information
4. Demographic info like gender, age group etc
5. Personal information such as email address.
Handling of collected information:-
We do not store any data at our side. The third party networks could 
use your data to show ads and analytic service. We do not guarantee how 
they handle data as those information is our of our control.
However, we can suggest that Third party networks are very concerned 
about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. They do not 
collect Personal Information, and they employ administrative, physical 
and electronic measures designed to protect your Non-Personal 
Information from unauthorized access and use. Please be aware that no 
security measures that we take to protect your information is absolutely
 guaranteed to avoid unauthorized access or use of your Non-Personal 
Information which is impenetrable.
We do not assure the exact data handling of these third party networks 
as this area is out of our control. To know better about their privacy 
policy look at this.
Use of application:-
If you have problem or suspect about any point of privacy policy. You 
can uninstall the app, as soon as you uninstall app we will not have any
 access of your data.
