Privacy Policy
Tachyon Protocol is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy.
This Privacy Policy (together with the Terms of Use and any other document referred to it) governs the manner in which the Company collects, uses, maintains and discloses any data collected from you, or provided by you to us. Please read the following carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your data and how we will treat it.
We do not collect logs of your activity, including no logging of browsing history, traffic destination, data content, or DNS queries. We do not process any data related to the online activity of our users.
We also take very seriously and put a lot of effort to ensure safe storage and protection of the user's Personal Data (as defined below).
Collection of information
Personal information
If you seek technical support via email, we will get your email address and the information you provided, but it will be deleted within 48 hours after handling the technical support. Namely, we DO NOT Collect any personal information except you seek technical support from us. Besides, We DO NOT Use, Sell or Disclose any personal information to third parties.
Diagnostics
To improve the quality of our products and enhance the user experience, we collect diagnostics information and crash reports on our apps, including connection events and error code. As you probably already understood, the data collected for diagnostic purposes does not contain uniquely identifiable information. This information will be deleted within 48 hours after handling the technical issues.
Apps and Apps versions
Number of daily active users and newly-added users. This information is collected when a user downloads the Tachyon VPN app or connects it. If you experience a connection bug, the Support Team may collect the information related to the Apps version, operating system, device type and time zone for troubleshooting.
