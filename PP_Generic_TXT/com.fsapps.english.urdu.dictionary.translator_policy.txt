SOME BASIC APPS PERMISSIONS AND THEIR USE:
Before using any of our applications, it is necessary to review the app permissions they require. You can find detailed information about the permissions in the application description on the app store. It is important to note that all permissions are strictly local, meaning your information is not used or shared outside the app. The permissions include Contact access, SdCard read and write access, Location access, and app accessibility service permission, among others.
Contact access is specifically used by the application for user operations involving contacts. SdCard permission is required for saving or editing photos. Location access allows the app to retrieve your current location. The app accessibility services permission is utilized solely for advanced protection and monitoring of the currently running applications, enhancing the functionality of the app locker. Rest assured that the aforementioned permissions are the only ones utilized for the purposes mentioned above.
