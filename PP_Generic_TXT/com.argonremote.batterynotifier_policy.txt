Privacy Policy (General)
This Privacy Policy details important information regarding the use and disclosure of User information collected through the Argon Dev Services. Argon Dev provides this Privacy Notice to help you make an informed decision about whether to use or continue using the Argon Dev Services.
This Privacy Notice is incorporated into and is subject to the Argon Dev Terms of Service. Your use of the Argon Dev Services and any personal information you provide on the Argon Dev Services remains subject to the terms of this Privacy Notice and the Terms of Service.
Terms and conditions
https://julietapp.blogspot.com/p/terms-and-conditions.html
APP DATA
All app data will be stored in your phone's internal storage. No third-party service can access this information. No information is public or stored in an online database.
If you uninstall the app, all stored data will be permanently deleted. If applicable, you can create a local backup copy of your data to restore it.
DATA COLLECTION
The app does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.
Link to the privacy policy of third party service providers used by the app:

ADVERTISING
The app uses AdMob to provide ads. AdMob is an advertising service provided by Google. AdMob can collect and use anonymous data to customize the ads for the user.
By default, data processing in AdMob is not restricted and personalized ads will be shown.
For users in EEA there is an additional dialog which allows to choose between personalized and non-personalized ads. The decision can be changed at any time. Just select the "Change or revoke consent" option from the main menu.
For users in California (CCPA), only non-personalized ads will be shown.
