Privacy Policy
Our Privacy Policy describes the information collected through our mobile applications
and the use
of the information.
We do not collect any personal information from children with our Apps.
When you download and use our mobile applications, we don’t require you to provide
any information
and we don’t collect any information about you or your device, except
for nonpersonal information about
the duration the Ap is used and how the App is used,
such as the screens viewed and actions taken
within our mobile applications.
We do not give out any nonpersonal information to anybody.
We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act.
Our website and mobile applications comply with the Children’s Online Privacy
Protection Act
