Permission android.permission.ACCESS_WIFI_STATE to access main feature of the app.
Permission android.permission.CHANGE_WIFI_STATE to access main feature of the app.
Permission android.permission.WRITE_SETTINGS to access main feature of the app.
Permission android.permission.BLUETOOTH to access main feature of the app.
Permission android.permission.BLUETOOTH_ADMIN to access main feature of the app.
Permission android.permission.KILL_BACKGROUND_PROCESSES to access main feature of the app.
Permission android.permission.INTERNET to access main feature of the app and improve user experience.
Permission android.permission.RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED to access main feature of the app.

MOLIBE VN

and its affiliates, shall be deemed that you have expressly provided your consent to ECO
