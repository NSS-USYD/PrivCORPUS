Privacy Policy
This Privacy policy is used to inform our users regarding our policies with the use, collection, and disclosure of Personal Information.The Personal Information that we collect are use for providing and improving the Services. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.
Informations Collection and Use
We will not collect any kind of your Personal informations.When you are using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information like screen resolution, device model, operating system version,and installed images in the applications. The information that we request is retained on your device and is not collected by us .
Link to privacy policy of third party service providers

AdMob
Facebook Ads
COOKIES
Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as unique identifiers.These are sent to your browser from the websites that you visit and are stored on your device internal memory.Our service does not use these cookies. However the apps may use third party code and libraries that use cookies to collect information and improve their services.You have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies.
Advertisement in Applications
We use advertisements in our applications. There could be errors in the programming and sometime programming errors may cause unwanted side effects.
Childerns Privacy Policy
We Randierinc will not collect the personal information of any children less than 14 years old. If the parents or guardians of any children find that the children have already provided their personal information without approval from them,they shall contact us and If we finds any personal information provided by children less than 13 years old,we will delete all related documents.
Changes to this Privacy Policy
Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time, we will post any privacy policy changes on this page, you are advised to review this page periodically.
