Privacy Policy
This is the privacy policy for the “Professional HD Camera” app.
• Professional HD Camera does not store, collect, send, and manage any personal data of any kind. All materials created by Professional HD Camera are solely yours (the user).
• Google Play requires us to disclose that Professional HD Camera will record camera sensor and microphone data, which used for the purpose of taking photos and recording videos.
• Location permission is required for geo-tagging of photos, but this feature is disabled by default. If you (the user) enabled it, your (the user) location is encoded in the saved image and video files (and it is only used for this purpose).
• Professional HD Camera uses “Google Admob” as ads provider. Google wants to show ads that are relevant to you. For more information, please check here.
• When manually requested by you (the user), Professional HD Camera sends the selected media by you (the user) from the device to a desired destination also selected by you (the user), such as email, social media, a different app, or other targets selected by you (the user). Under this operation, Professional HD Camera also does not store, collect, send, and manage any personal data of any kind.
• When manually requested by you (the user), Professional HD Camera sends the app's log by you (the user) from the device to Professional HD Camera support email. Under this operation Professional HD Camera also does not store, collect, send, manage any personal data of any kind.
