Fantastic Droid – Privacy and Information Security Policy
Thank You for choosing to use Our Products and services. We respect the privacy of every individual who uses our services. We do not collect information that may be used to identify individuals.

If there should arise questions or dissatisfaction regarding this policy, then please email us.
