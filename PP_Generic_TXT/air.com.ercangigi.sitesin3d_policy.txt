Sites in VR - Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
Personal Data
We do not collect any personal data (like your name, address, phone number, etc.).
Non-Personal Data
The 'Sites in VR' app may collect non personal data like your devices hardware properties or in app choices like the chosen VR viewer.
This information is used for driving statistics that will be in turn used to improve the app.
We will not use this data to identify you in any way.
This data will not be disclosed to third parties.
What is the camera used for?
The 'Sites in VR' app uses the camera only to scan a QR code with which the user can configure a VR viewer.
There is no other information extracted from the camera images other than the QR code.
The camera images are not stored or shared in any way.
Are there other sources that collect data?
This website uses Google Analytics for driving statistcs, please see their
Privacy Policy
for more details.
The 'Sites in VR' app has no thirt party componets that collect data.
Changes
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason.
As we do not collect any personal information, including your contact information,
